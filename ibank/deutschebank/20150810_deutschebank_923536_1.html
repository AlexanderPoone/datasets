THEME OF THE DAY

China Property - Pre-positioning for more supportive policies and improving supply picture (Tony Tsang)

Since stock market corrections in late May, valuations of Chinese developers have returned to levels at end-1Q15, and the sector now trades at NAV discount (39% market-cap weighted), PE (7.Ox) and PB (0.9x) that are all close to " 1SD” of historical ranges. In the physical market, residential sales volume has remained solid YoY and should sequentially pick up starting Sep as new launches turn active. Also, we expect more supportive government policies given weak macro, while demand-supply in property market should continue to improve (inventory situations normalizing and future new supply falling), which should lead to better pricing power for good projects. Buy quality SOE names. (Tony Tsang - 852 2203 6256) http://pull.db-qmresearch.com/pZ1818-7420/12479421/0900b8c08a070946.pdf

China Property - Anticipate soft earnings in upcoming interim results; Buy SOE developers (Tony Tsang)

We anticipate the upcoming interim result announcements for Chinese developers to be rather soft in general, with gross margins likely to see further compression. On the other hand, we expect net gearing for many developers to rise, despite the low level of land acquisitions in 1H15. Meanwhile, while we have already seen three companies (Franshion, Joy City and Beijing Capital Land) issue profit warnings, we believe these are exceptional cases on uneven revenue bookings. In our view, while the soft results could dampen market sentiment, this provides a good entry opportunity for state-owned developers like COLI, CR Land, Greentown, Joy City and Franshion. (Tony Tsang - 852 2203 6256) http://pull.db-gmresearch.eom/p/64-2C63LW783703I0900b8_c08a05ac47.pdf

China Dairy Sector - What if raw milk price comes down by 10%? (Winnie Mak)

Fonterra cut its farmgate milk price forecast by 27%, and we see 10% downside on China raw milk, based on a stable corn price. We still prefer downstream, which has a track record of margin expansion despite a falling raw milk price. (Winnie Mak- 852 2203 6187) http://pull.db-qmresearch.eom/p/1324-4AF0/11451405/0900b8c08a05a138.pdf
