From generous to very generous; maintaining Buy

CRE announced CRH's revised bidding offer; trading to resume today

  • 1)    The offer to its non beer business to raise from HK$28bn (HKS11.5/share) to HKS30bn (HKS12.3/share), due to change in market condition since its original offer in April 15. The loss from the disposal will be ~ HK$5bn.

  • 2)    Special dividend raised from HK$11.5 to HK$12.3/ shares, in due course

  • 3)    A3 year HK$10bn loan (maximum) from CRH to CRE at market rate. This is to illustrate CRH’s continue support and commitment to CRE, including potential future organic or in organic growth opportunities in its beer business

  • 4)    The maximum number of share under pre conditional partial offer increase from 242m (10% of issued share) to 484m (2 0%) at HK$12.7/share (unchanged)

The total revised offer increased from HKS24.2 to HK$25 per share. Dispatch of the circular will be on or before 7 Oct 2015.


Deutsche Bank view

The revised offer is a positive surprise to us and we believe market will take it positively.

■ The revised offer for the non beer business represents 14% discount to book as of 2014.


■ Meanwhile, the HK$10bn loan to CRE is new to us as we believe the remaining beer operation will be active in consolidating the market with additional funding.


■ The increase in the partial offer will shrink the free float from 48% before the offer to 28% post offer.


We maintain our Buy rating.
