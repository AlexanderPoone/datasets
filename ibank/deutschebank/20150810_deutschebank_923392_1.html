South China Moring Post: China Has Approved SOE Plan (Hexun, Aug 10) China was said to have approved the plan for SOE reforms, which may copy the model of Temasek to allow more flexibility in decision making and human resource management in SOEs, according to media report.

CSRC Asked Brokers to Continue Maintaining Stability (CSRC, Aug 7)

China Securities Regulatory Commission recently held meeting with brokers and fund managers and asked them to maintain market satiability, according to CSRC spokesman Deng Ge at a regular briefing on last Friday. The regulator asked brokers to strengthen management on programming trading and not to use it for malicious short selling. In addition, CSRC reiterated that brokers must not provide margin lending through non-compliant channels.

CGS July Unconsolidated Net Profit down 31% mom to Rmb987mn (The Company, Aug 7)

China Galaxy Securities announced on Aug 7 that it recorded company-level net profit of Rmb987mn for July 2015, down by 31% mom, according to a statement to HKEx.

PBOC Released 2Q15 Monetary Policy Report (PBOC, Aug 7)

The People's Bank of China says in its 2Q15 monetary policy report on last Friday that it will let the market play a larger role in deciding exchange rate and facilitate more flexibility in the two-way movement of RMB. The central bank reiterated that it will leverage multiple policy tools to ensure appropriate liquidity supply and provide an adaptive environment for the economic restructuring, according to Friday's statement. PBOC also said it will hold on the bottom line of ''preventing regional and systemic financial risks".
