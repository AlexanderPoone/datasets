Weekly sales down 15.5% WoW, back to the normal level seen in July

Foo Leung

Research Associate (+852 ) 2203 6239 foo.leung@db.com


During the week of 3 Aug to 9 Aug 2015, total sales volume in the 40 major cities dropped 15.5% WoW to 5.982 msqm, also representing a 0.4% MoM decline. Weekly sales in the last week were up 35.2% YoY. After a strong week at end-July, weekly sales have returned to the normal level seen in July. The average sales volume in the first three weeks in July was 6.1 msqm, similar to the 6.0msqm last week. 2015 YTD, sales grew 20.1% YoY to 182.6 msqm. ASP for the week was RMB10,717 psqm, representing changes of -0.88% WoW/-0.58% MoM/+3.46% YoY.

Sales volume in Tier 1 cities dropped 16.5% WoW

Sales volume in Tier 1 cities decreased 16.5% WoW to 906 ksqm during the week. Total sales volume in Tier 1 cities was -24.8% MoM and +48.0% YoY. YTD sales volume in Tier 1 cities was up 55% YoY. ASP in Tier 1 cities last week was recorded at RMB23,851 psqm, up 25% YoY.

Sales volume in Tier 2 cities decreased 16.4% WoW

During the week, sales volume in Tier 2 cities decreased 16.4% WoW to 4,101 msqm. Tier 2 cities' sales volume was up 9% MoM and 31% YoY. YTD sales volume in Tier 2 cities was up 11.9% YoY. ASP in Tier 2 cities last week was recorded at RMB10,073 psqm, down 0.4% YoY.

Sales volume in Tier 3 cities down 10.9% WoW

For Tier 3 cities, sales volume decreased 10.9% WoW to 976 ksqm. Sales volume in Tier 3 cities was down 7% MoM and up 43% YoY. YTD sales volume in Tier 3 cities increased 28% YoY. ASP in Tier 3 cities last week was recorded at RMB7,405 psqm, down 4.2% YoY.
