CSRC Said to Resume Reviews of Secondary Stock Offering on Friday

Top Stories

CSRC Said to Resume Reviews of Secondary Stock Offering on Friday (Hexun, Aug 6)

CSRC is said to resume reviews of secondary stock offering and the review will mainly focus on projects which have already passed preliminary examinations, local media reported. Additionally, IPO reviews will not be resumed currently, according to people close to the regulator.

CSRC's Zhang Yujun May Become PBOC Vice Governor (Tencent, Aug 5) Zhang Yujun, Assistant Chairman of CSRC, may be named as Vice Governor of PBOC and his transition is still pending internal evolutions, local media reported, citing unidentified people familiar with the matter. In addition, Li Chao, Deputy Director of PBOC's State Administration of Foreign Exchange, may become Vice Chairman of CSRC and his transition has already been approved by internal PBOC vote, according to the report.

Everbright Securities Announces 1H15 NPAT of Rmb4.88bn, Up 1180% yoy (The Company, Aug 5)

Everbright Securities on Wednesday announced its revenue and NPAT for 1H15 were Rmb9.52bn and Rmb4.88bn, up 395% and 1180% yoy, respectively. The net capital at company level was Rmb20.99bn as of 1H15, compared with Rmb16.90bn at end-2014, according to a statement to SHEx. In addition, the company said that it has obtained approval from CSRC for issuing additional A-shares in private placement.

Number of New Investors in China Stock Market Hits Record Low Since May 2015 (CSDC, Aug 5)

The number of new investors in China stock market was 340.5K for the week from July 27 to July 31 2015, down 13% wow, which is the lowest level since record in May 2015, according to data released by China Securities Depository and Clearing Corporation (CSDC).

Most Chinese Banks Still Keep Leverage Ratio for Stock Financing Business at 1:2 (NBD, Aug 6)

Most Chinese banks have tightened risk controls for lending to stock investment after the stock market slump recently, while still keeping leverage ratio at 1:2 for such business, local media reported.

PBOC Withdraws Net Liquidity of Rmb5bn from the Market This Week (PBOC, Aug 6)

PBOC sold Rmb35bn of 7-day reverse repos at a yield of 2.5% on Thursday, according to the statement posted on PBOC's website. With Rmb90bn reverse repos matured and Rmb50bn reverse repos sold on Tuesday, PBOC made net liquidity withdrawal of Rmb5bn from market this week.

MOF to Roll over Rmb30bn of 5-Y Bonds on August 12 (MOF, Aug 5)

MOF will roll over Rmb30bn of 5-years bonds on August 12 2015, according to the statement posted on MOF's website.
