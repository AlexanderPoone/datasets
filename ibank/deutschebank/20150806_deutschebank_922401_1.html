Annualized cost savings of US$50m identified

MPEL’s 2Q property EBITDA of US$205m was 6% below consensus. But mgmt said this included a US$5m impact from low VIP hold and another US$20m impact from very-low premium mass hold. Thus, luck-adj EBITDA was US$230m (-7% qoq, -33% yoy), in line with consensus. Luck-adj margin was flat qoq at 24%. Importantly, mgmt identified cost saving opportunities worth an annualized US$50m with benefits starting in 2H15. Aside from cuts in mktg spend & corp expenses, a key part would be the transfer of staff (mainly dealers) from City of Dreams to Studio City which opens on 27th October. On back of this cost initiative, we lift 2016 EBITDA by 5%, and TP from US$18 to US$20. A key risk is an insufficient table quota at Studio City.

20 results highlights

  • ■    Studio City table quota still in discussion: Mgmt said yesterday that they are still in discussion with govt for 400 gaming tables. Our channel checks suggest they may receive only approx. 150 tables (110 tables for mass, 40 for premium mass and direct VIP). Studio City has many non-gaming offerings, includingl ,600 hotel rooms (vs. COD: 1,400 rooms), a ferris wheel, an entertainment center, and a Batman virtual reality ride.

  • ■    Manila lifted by strong junket ramp-up: COD Manila beat expectations, generating 2Q EBITDA of US$12.6m (1Q: US$3.0m, luck adj US$9.0m) with margin now at 16.8%. This came from a strong ramp-up in junket roll to US$496m, +160% qoq, as two large junkets (including Suncity) started in May. Another large junket is expected to start late Aug/early Sept. Mass rev +15% qoq to US$30m. Hotel occ improved to 89% from 75% in 1Q.

  • ■    City of Dreams hurt by bad luck: 2Q EBITDA fell 19% qoq, (-32% yoy) to US$179m on lower VIP roll (-18% qoq) and bad luck on both VIP and mass (VIP hold 2.7% vs. 1Q 3.2%, Mass hold 32.4% vs. 1Q 35.9%). Mass drop was flat qoq but mass table rev fell 10% qoq given very bad luck at premium mass. Mgmt said luck had reverted back to normal in July. Given bad luck, mass table yield slipped 12% qoq (-31% yoy) to US$12.6k/day, though still highest in Macau. ADR -4% qoq ( + 1% qoq) to US$198 with occ stable at 99% as over 90% of rooms are comped to players.
