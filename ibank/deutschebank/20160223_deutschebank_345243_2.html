EM Daily: Updating fiscal breakevens for EM oil producers

Argentina: Order to (conditionally) lift injunctions reinforces our bullish view In a dramatic change of event, US District Court Judge Thomas Griesa issued a court order on Friday to lift the existing injunctions barring Argentina from paying exchange bond holders before they pay the claims of the pari-passu plaintiffs subject to certain conditions. In our view, the negotiating power has dramatically changed to Argentina's favor with this order by Judge Griesa. This order should help facilitate an even faster normalization of Argentina's debt situation, a scenario in favor of Argentina assets in general, and the global bonds in particular. For more details, see our Trade Recommendation note.
