New Zealand prices challenged by increased European exports

On 7 August 2015, Fonterra Co-operative cut its farmgate milk price forecast by 27% to to NZ$3.85 per kgMS from NZ$5.25 kgMS (forecast made on 28 May 2015) for the 2015/16 season, and also reduced its milk volume forecast by 2% for the period to 1,589m kgMS. Earlier, Fonterra's GDT auction held on 4 August 2015 showed a 9.3% decrease in the overall GDT Price Index, the tenth consecutive decline, with skimmed milk powder (SMP) and whole milk powder (WMP) prices falling 10.3% and 14.4%, respectively, from the prior auction held on 15 July.
