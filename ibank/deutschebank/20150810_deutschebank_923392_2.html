China CPI grew by 1.6% yoy and 0.3% mom in July 2015, partly driven by the increase in pork price, according to data released by National Statistics Bureau.

PBOC China End-July Forex Reserves at USD3.65tr (PBOC, Aug 7)

China's foreign exchange reverses stood at USD3.65tr as of July-end, the lowest level since August 2013, according to data disclosed by PBOC on its website.

CSRC to Inspect Internet Platforms Offering Equity Financing (CSRC, Aug 7) China Securities Regulatory Commission will carry out a special inspection on internet platforms offering equity financing, media reported. Companies and individuals are not allowed to raise equity financing through these platforms unless approved by the securities regulator.
