Strong contracted sales in the low season

Strong July contracted sales with 584% YoY surge

Franshion announced its contracted sales in July amounted to RMB2.72bn, representing a 584% YoY surge and a 19% MoM decline. During the month, GFA of 100ksqm have been sold, up 581% YoY and down 49% MoM. ASP for the month was RMB27,168psqm, flat 0% YoY and up 59% MoM due to product mix. The monthly sales value in July was third highest in 2015 so far.

In 7M15, total contracted sales of Franshion were up 89% YoY to RMB13.0bn, with GFA sold up 125% YoY to 0.61 msqm. ASP for the period declined 16% YoYtoRMB20,332psqm.

The luxury project in Shanghai contributed to the strong sales in the month as well as the increase in ASP. Shanghai Daning Jin Mao Palace recorded over RMBI.Obn sales in July. After the two rounds of launch, there are not many unsold units left in this project. The project in Beijing was another major sales contributor for the month.

Next round of major launching is expected in Sep-Oct

In the first half of the year, Franshion has been focusing on the residential projects. There are still lands and commercial projects planning to be launched in the second half of 2015. The commercial project, Shanghai International Shipping Services Center will be launched in 2H. The ASP of the project in 2014 was RMB94,800 psqm. The company expects the major launches in 2H will be on September/October.

Sellable resources this year is more evenly distributed

Among the RMB40bn sellable resources for 2015, the distribution is more evenly distributed with about 50:50 for 1H:2H, compared to the heavily back-loaded schedule in 2014. According to the company, total sellable resources launched in 1H15 were about R MB20bn.

Attractive valuations at 53% NAV discount and 0.59x P/B

Our TP of HK$3.72 is based on 30% discount to our NAV of HK$5.31. Currently the stock is trading at 53% discount to NAV and 0.59x 2015E PB. Franshion's valuations are attractive in our view. Risks: unexpected economic and policy volatility.
