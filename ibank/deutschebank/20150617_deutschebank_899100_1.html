CRE announces 1Q15 results - Excluding asset revaluation, CRE's core net profit was up 3.2% to HKD360m on sales increase of 16.3% to HKD49bn.

Beer business recorded a net profit increase of 750% to HKD51m on sales increase of 8.0% to HKD8.5bn. This was driven by sales volume increase of 3% due to enhanced production capacity and improved marketing and sales channels. Among the categories, mid- and high-end products sales volume was up 30%, and premium beer sales momentum also boosted the overall sales volume. ASP grew 5.6% in 1Q15.

Retail core net profit declined 27.8% (excluding after-tax revaluation surplus and disposal of non-core assets), while sales grew 21.3% to HKD34bn. Tesco JV recorded a net loss of HKD79m on sales of HKD5.2bn. Its retail business posted SSS decline of 3.3%.

Food business suffered a net loss of HKD38m in 1Q15 vs. a net loss of HKD50m in 1Q14. Sales grew 3.9% to HKD4.1bn. The food business was affected by business transition.


Beverage business saw a 560% increase in net profit and 6.2% growth in sales.

Sales volume rose 9.3%, mainly driven by significant growth in water sales.
