The announcement didn't say anythjng, but we think...

Group level is aiming for strategic reorganization

CITS (601888.SS, Buy, TP RMB65) and China Travel (0308.HK, Buy, TP HKD4.8) announced today that their parent companies, CITS Group Corporation (CITS Group) and China National Travel Service (HK) Group Corporation (HKCTS Group), are in discussion on strategic reorganization between the two Group companies.

In the short term, both stocks may rebound

We believe market may react positively on the news in the short term. The SOE reform from the group level may indicate that management from the listed companies may have more incentive after the restructure. CITS and China Travel are currently trading at 39% and 33% discount to their one year historical high, respectively: 18x 2016 PER (ex-cash) for CITS and 10x 2016 PER (ex-cash) for China Travel.

In the long term, we believe CITS will benefit more

We believe the mapping for the business nature between CITS, China Travel (308 HK) and China Travel Group is very clear: 1) CITS will focus on domestic duty free; 2) China Travel will focus on tourism site operation and 3) China Travel Group will focus on travel agency. As a result, we believe the restructure will focus on CITS's travel agency business which barely contributes any earnings to the listed company. As result, if the travel agency is restructured, i.e. taking over by the group level, CITS could focus more on the domestic duty free business and thus its ROE can be improved and valuation will re-rate.______________________________________________________________ ¡Figure 1: What we think after restructuring
