More recapitalization exercises expected

Be selective

In our report, "China Prop: High likelihood of recapitalization in May-July; be selective", of May 6, we argued that potential share placements presented an overhang for highly geared developers and that there had been a high amount of recapitalization exercises during previous bull markets. Since then, 11 listed developers (e.g. CR Land, Evergrande, Franshion, CIFI, and SZI) have opted for recapitalization, raising about US$3.6bn. For the next 3-4 weeks, we expect more such recapitalization activities for other developers, before the 1H15 results blackout periods start in July. We advocate being selective and remain cautious on developers with high gearing or aggressive acquisition records, such as Sunac, Evegrande, and Shimao.
