Summary Investment Thesis and Valuation

Despite macro uncertainties, which in our view have already been priced into investor expectations, we think Alibaba's cost optimization initiatives will continue to drive profit upside in the coming quarters. We believe the bottom-line growth rate bottomed out in June quarter and will accelerate to 35%+ YoY in March quarter 2023, driven by 1) a recovery of highly profitable domestic ecommerce revenue (CMR), and 2) cost optimization across a large number of operations. We expect earnings revision to turn positive post June quarter and to serve as a key catalyst in the next few quarters.

Valuation

Our Jun-23 PT of HK$140 (after rounding) is based on a target multiple of 15x CY2023E P/E, to see through the near-term impact from the lockdowns. We believe 15x is fair given our forecasted CY23-25E adjusted EPS CAGR of 20%.
