Koc

Holding

Best-in-class example of a corporate holding in Turkey, where underlying companies are managed by professionals, and family members appear only on the board level. Family members are in harmony and no major issues known to the public.

4 main sectors (Finance, Auto, Oil & gas refining/retai I, Consumer durables) and diversified other

Yapi Kredi Ford Otosan Tofas Otokar Otokoc Turk Traktor Tupras Aygaz Opet Entek Arcelik

Group enjoys the scale advantage in almost all its main sectors through its #1 market share companies. Koc owns the only refiner in Turkey (Tupras), 2 biggest auto companies of the country (Ford Otosan and Tofas), #1 Consumer durables company (Arcelik) and a well-established bank with #4 rank in total banking assets among private banks & #1 share in credit cards. The group is a close proxy to the Turkish consumer story and well positioned to capture the growth of Turkish economy.

Koc Holding operates one of the biggest distribution networks in the white goods sector and autos. Dealer network is independent and has no ownership relationship with Koc Holding. The credit risk, profitability is all under the dealer’s responsibility while Koc only monitors them and supply some margin that they can operate. Their supplier network is diversified and they do not own their suppliers except refinery business which is vertically integrated.

They recently allocated more capital to Yapi Kredi Bank together with Unicredit in order to strengthen its capital base. They continue to divest some small assets that were classified previously as Assets Available For Sale.

Sabanci

Holding

A strong corporate structure, transforming fast from family management into professional management Holding is currently chaired by the third generation Sabanci family member Guler Sabanci. Sabanci family is a big one and not as united as the Koc family. Second generation Sevket Sabanci chose to start his own business (Esas holding) in 2000.

6 main sectors (Banking, Insurance, Industrials, Cement Energy, Retail) and diversified other

Group’s underlying companies are some of the largest in their respective sectors and cash generative, similar to Koc holding companies. Holding has leading market shares in key sectors: #1 in Cement, Tire&Tire reinforcement, Electronics retail, Private electricity generation &distribution businesses, #2 in private pensions, #5 in Organized food retail. Meanwhile, Akbank (which forms -40% of the group’s NAV) is ranked #3 in loan volumes & #1 or #2 in profitability in the past 20 years.

Following the IPOs of AvivaSA and EnerjiSA, transparency on NAV has improved substantially, where listed companies together with net cash positioning share in total NAV increased to 92% from 70%.

Sabanci's dealer network is small due to the sectors it operates. They work with different suppliers in retail and have strong relationship. However, all suppliers are independent.
