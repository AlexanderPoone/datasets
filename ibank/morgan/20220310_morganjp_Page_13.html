Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Shoals Technologies Group (Overweight Price Target: $37.00)

Investment Thesis

Maintain Overweight rating. SHLS trades at ~25x our FY22E EV/EBITDA. We believe the multiple, which is a significant discount to our projected EBITDA CAGR of ~50%, is compelling.

Valuation

Our Dec 2022 price target is $37, based on an assigned EV/EBITDA multiple of 35x our FY23 EBITDA estimate. We believe FY23E is more suited for valuation, as it reflects ramping sales internationally and market adjacencies. The assigned multiple represents a PEG ratio of ~0.7x our forecast of ~50% EBITDA growth, slightly below the ratio for ENPH and SEDG.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

End-market solar demand could fall short of expectations if the economy slows, austerity programs are imposed, the cost of capital increases, or government policy imposes constraints on deployment activity.

Competition could arise, whether new entrants or large multi-industry companies that develop workarounds to replicate Shoals’s success, though without infringing on patents.

The company could fail to execute, possibly owing to the demands put on the organization as it expands the product portfolio, moves into new market verticals, and goes international. Control and quality issues could arise, and unanticipated investments might weigh on cash flow relative to expectations.

Loss of one or more key customers could damage the brand and lead to loss of market share, particularly if the loss originates in product quality and safety issues.
