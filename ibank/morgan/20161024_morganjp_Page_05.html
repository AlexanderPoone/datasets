Asia Pacific Equity Research 24 October 2016


JPMorgan


Downside risks include: (1) a downturn in the retail environment in Hong Kong and Macau; (2) a downturn in Mainland Chinese visitors into Hong Kong and Macau; (3) lower-than-expected gross margins through increased price reinvestment or promotional activity; (4) weakness in in-store execution; (5) changes in government regulations that affect the price difference of cosmetic products between Mainland China and Hong Kong; and (6) higher-than-expected rental and staff cost pressure.
