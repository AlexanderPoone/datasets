Financial review

BVPS retreated modestly on FX loss and investment revaluation loss

Despite the small positive revaluation of investment properties at HK$525m, the company’s book value declined slightly by 0.3% HoH to HK$101.3 due to FX loss and revaluation loss on investments. The valuation of Harbour City decreased by about HK$0.2bn (0.1% of property value).

Dividend outlook to somewhat tie with IP performance

Management highlighted that for the past 10 years the dividend payment has had a relationship with IP performance. With IP core profit growing by 11% YoY in 1H, the company increased the interim dividend by 5.5%. A full-year dividend increase would depend how economic uncertainties and the company’s operations evolve over the next six months.

Gearing remains stable with capacity for new investments

Net debt to total equity edged up slightly from 14.9% in December 2015 to 15.2% in June 2016. The company still has capacity to make new investments in HK and China.
