Approximately three quarters of IMAX China’s revenue is contributed by the theatre business, which involves charging fees to exhibitors for providing IMAX theatre systems, brand and technology licensing and maintenance sendees, etc. The company adopts three types of arrangements, namely: 1) sales arrangements, 2) hybrid revenue-sharing; and 3) full revenue sharing. They differ in the required amount of upfront payment and box office sharing obligation. The company intends to expand the proportion of revenue-sharing arrangements, which in our view, provide higher long-term returns compared to the sales arrangement by allowing the company to charge a percentage of the box office revenues over a period of 10 to 12 years.
