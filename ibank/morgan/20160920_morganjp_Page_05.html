Lifting NP on stronger pricing

We have revised FYI6 and FYI7 sales up 1.8 and 2.4 percent respectively to reflect stronger laminates, chemicals and PCB demand. We assume an increase of 1.1-1.2% in GPM as selling prices rise faster than the cost of production. The increase in NP of 8.4% in FYI6 and 11.8% in FYI7 is mainly due to higher prices and GPM for all core operations. We lift our DCF based Dec-17 PT to HK$32 (prev. HK$27).
