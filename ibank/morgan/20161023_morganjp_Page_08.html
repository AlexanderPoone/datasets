Lowering wheel loader sales forecasts

Lowering FY16/17 forecast for wheel loaders to -12%/4% Y/Y. We have seen a divergence in demand growth for wheel loaders and excavators YTD, partly driven by the trend of some wheel loaders being substituted with small-sized excavators, which has dampened new loader demand. Even though YTD growth for wheel loaders remains negative, we have seen the pace of decline narrow in recent months and anticipate this trend to continue in light of the rebound in overall C/E demand and on-the-ground activity. Looking ahead, we expect to see an inflection point for wheel loader sales growth in FYI7.
