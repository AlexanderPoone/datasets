Weekly sales & policy update

  • •    60-city weekly sales (more): For the week ending 28 May, sales grew 8% w/w to a total of 40K units, with a mild y/y decline of 2%. Sales are still 41% below the 4-year average (last week: -41%), which is within the average of -40-50% in May and shows no turnaround yet.

  • •    Local-level policy update (full summary): There was some easing on HPF mortgage over the past week, with Tianjin allowed to follow the 1st home policy after the HPF mortgage is fully repaid, and Hengyang increased maximum loan amount to Rmb800K.
