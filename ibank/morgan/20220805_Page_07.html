Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Alibaba Group Holding Limited (Overweight; Price Target: $145.00)

Investment Thesis

Despite macro uncertainties, which in our view have already been priced into investor expectations, we think Alibaba's cost optimization initiatives will continue to drive profit upside in the coming quarters. We believe the bottom-line growth rate bottomed out in June quarter and will accelerate to 35%H- YoY in March quarter 2023, driven by 1) a recovery of highly profitable domestic ecommerce revenue (CMR), and 2) cost optimization across a large number of operations. We expect earnings revision to turn positive post June quarter and to serve as a k⑶ catalyst in the next few quarters.

Valuation

Our Jun-2 3 PT of US$145 is based on 15x CY2023E P/E, to see through the near-term impact from the lockdowns. We believe 15x is fair given our forecasted CY23-25E adjusted EPS CAGR of 20%.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

K⑶ downside risks include: 1) large Chinese internet companies such as Tencent and Baidu could pose a threat to Alibaba's Local Services business; 2) investments ■ in digital content could be a long-lasting effort and bring margin pressure over the long term; and 3) the progress of mobile monetization improvement might be slower than expected, along with the sustainability of China5s retail marketplace GMV and revenue growth in the longer run.
