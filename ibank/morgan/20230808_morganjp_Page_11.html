Depleted Uranium

Sustainalytics uses a broad definition of DU, i.e. with U-235 concentrations of 0.7%or lower. The current findings of Sustainalytics would not have been different if the company had used a narrower definition given that there is in practice not much debate about classifying DU ammunition. There is currently no convention or legal framework lllegalising or regulating depleted uranium.

Nuclearweapons

As the 1968 Treaty on the Non-Proliferation of NuclearWeapons (NPT) does not contain a definition, Sustainalytics bases its definition of a nuclear weapon on the Treaty for the Prohibition of Nuclearweapons in Latin America and the Caribbean (Tlatelolco).

http://disarmament.un.Org/treaties/t/tlatelolco

White Phosphorus

AWP munition is any projectile (e.g. flares, grenades, or mortars) that is equipped with WP, in order to act as a smoke-producing agent, or as tracer, illumination, or incendiary munition. There is currently no convention or legal framework comprehensively illegalizing or regulating white phosphorus.
