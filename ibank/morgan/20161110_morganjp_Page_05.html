Rich valuation for low growth business

HKCG has historically enjoyed a valuation premium over its HK utilities’ peers because it was the company delivering the highest earnings growth of >20%, driven by its China gas distribution business. However, following the company's disappointing results in 2015, with operating profit after tax down 2% yoy, we expect HKCG's future earnings growth to be at a mid-single digit growth rate.
