Valuation

DCF-based Dec-21 PT of HKS1.15

Our Dec-21 price target of HK$ 1.15 is based on a DCF valuation that assumes an equity risk premium of 7.0% and a risk-free rate of 4%. We also assume a beta of 1.3 and debt-to-capital ratio of 10%, which results in a WACC of 12.1%.
