Nominal Index Extensions

Based on price and modified duration data as of September 22, we estimate month-end index extensions for Bloomberg government bond indices of G4 countries, Australia, and New Zealand.

Euro area

O.O37y extension in the overall index; Finland extends most

Broad euro government bond index: We expect an extension of O.O37y, lower than an average September extension of 0.084y and an average monthly extension (0.079y) -

Exhibit?. A total of about €87 billion of issuance will affect the extension and about €42.6 billion worth of market value of bonds will fall out of the index.

Exhibit 4 summarises the relative changes in index duration by issuer and maturity sector for euro sovereigns this month. By sector, we see the largest extension of 0.069y in the 10-15y sector and, country-wise, we expect Finland to extend the most at O.25y, followed by Germany at 0.081y. In addition, there will be notable sector-wise extensions in Finland and Italy, due to bonds changing indices.
