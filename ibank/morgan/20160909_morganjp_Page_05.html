Fuyao-H

N

21.6

21.0

Our Jun-17 PT of HK$21 is based on a 13.5x P/E, at the higher end of Fuyao’s historical average or the global component valuation range. We believe Fuyao will trade at a premium to global auto parts, driven by its global leadership (i.e., second-largest in the world and the largest in China) and much higher yield or dividend payout vs. component peers we cover globally.

Upside and downside risks include: (1) Russian exposure, as Fuyao has 4.0mn sq.m auto glass capacity in Russia (~4%of its current global auto glass capacity) and another 8.1mn sq.m under construction, scheduled to be completed by end-16 -uncertainty around Russia’s economy, despite its being the second-largest auto market in Europe, and its currency could be swing factors to Fuyao’s profit; and (2) raw material prices, including energy, are important factors for Fuyao’s margin -fluctuations in energy prices are a potential risk to profitability.
