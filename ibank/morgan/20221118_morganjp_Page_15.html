Which industries are impacted by upgrades/downgrades in 2022? The Gaming/Leisure industry is the most impacted by loan upgrades (29) in 2022 followed by Tech (23) and Industrials (18). The Healthcare industry is the most impacted by loan downgrades (42) in 2022 followed by Consumer Products (31), Technology (29), and Services (24). Which rating category is the most impacted by positive agency trends? The upgrade-to-downgrade ratio by number for the Leveraged Loan market is 0.77:1 in 2022, which for BB, B, and CCC issuers is 1.12:1, 0.67:1, and 0.87:1. Note the upgrade-to-downgrade ratio in 2021 for BB, B, and CCC issuers was 1.97:1, 1.86:1, and 2.40:1. Analyzing the rating buckets of the Leveraged loan index, which has decreased and increased by the most since the beginning of 2021 in terms of Moodys? The market-weight of Caal (2.3%) and B3 (9.6%) loans in the index is down 180bp and 192bp since the beginning of 2021, respectively, which compares to an increase of 243bp in B2 (33.8%). Note the Caal portion of the loan index has grown by the most (+43bp) during the past six months’ downgrade wave.
