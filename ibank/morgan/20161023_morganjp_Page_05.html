Improving real estate investment outlook

We further note the improving leading indicators for real estate investment are consistent with the strong recovery in excavator sales and construction truck sales since July.

We highlight the sequential improvement in land sales in the last couple of quarters, based on which we expect new starts growth, as well as real estate investment growth, to re-accelerate from November. Refer to Figure 2 below - new starts correlate strongly with residential land sales with a 4-month time lag.

Although land sales slipped in September on a high base, we expect land supply to increase in October as more tier-2 cities roll out more land for sale as a way to tame price growth. Despite the recent tightening measures announced in 20+ cities, given the low inventory levels, we expect sales momentum to remain strong in coming months.
