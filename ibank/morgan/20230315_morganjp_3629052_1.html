iTraxx Europe Series 39

Introducing the new European CDS Indices

  • •    iTraxx indices roll into their new “on-the-run” series, which are to start trading on Monday, 20 March 2023. This note details the differences between iTraxx Series 39 and Series 38 (Table 1 and Table 2), with the latter having a new composition and additional six months of maturity. Our expectations of the effect of these differences on the traded spreads are shown in Table 3.

  • •    We introduce the most liquid indices and describe their main characteristics, as well as the differences between the new “on-the-run” series and its predecessors in terms of maturities and composition. We review rating and spread distributions and country exposures of each index and provide our expectations of the fair value and traded spreads of the new indices.

  • •    We also provide expected trading levels for the corresponding options.
