Key catalysts for the stock price:

Upcoming sales and earnings release. Any signs of improvement in sales performance will be taken as a positive while any signs of continued slowdown in sales growth will likely be taken as a negative catalyst. Any positive development of Sa Sa cross border ecommerce business and 020 Initiative will likely be taken positively.

Upside risks to our view:

  • (1)    Any upturn In Mainland Chinese tourism in Hong Kong ;

  • (2)    Any upturn in customer purchasing behavior within Sa Sa stores;

  • (3)    Lower than expected operating expenses ;

  • (4)    Better than expected company execution at store level;

  • (5)    Better than expected cross border ecommerce and 020 Initiatives

Downside risks to ourview:

  • (1)    Downturn in the retail environment in Hong Kong and Macau;

  • (2)    Downturn in Mainland Chinese visitors into Hong Kong and Macau;

  • (3)    Lower than expected gross margins through increased orice reinvestment or promotional activity.

  • (4)    Weakness In In-store execution for Sa Sa;

  • (5)    Change in government regulations that impact the orice difference of cosmetics products in Mainland China relative to Hong Kong;

  • (6)    Higher than expected rental and staff cost pressure
