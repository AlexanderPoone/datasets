Investment Thesis

We maintain Neutral as we believe expected weak retail numbers are already in the price and retail losses are likely to narrow in FY15 as management is confident about savings on the back of integration as well as closing down loss-making stores. For FY15 we expect CRE to report HK$313mn recurring profit vs HK$794mn recurring net loss reported in FYI4. We will monitor two developments to turn more positive on the name: i) Tesco integration and savings related to this being on track or better than expected and ii) improvement in beer sales momentum.

Valuation

Our Jun-16 PT is HK$ 15.80, based on our sum-of-the-parts valuation, in which we assign: 1) 6x EV/ EBITDA to FYI 6E Retail discounted to FY15; 2) 22x P/E for Beer, 3) 15x PE for beverages; 4) lOx P/E for food and 5) lOx P/E forproperty.
