Raising Weichai and industry forecasts for FY16/17E

We reset our FY16/17 industry forecasts for excavators to +ll%/+6% (prior +5%/+6%), for HDTs to +19%/+7% (prior +4%/+5%), and for wheel loaders to -12%/+4% (prior +l%/+5%). Our revisions reflect YTD sales trends, with excavator and HDT demand recovery surging ahead of expectations while loader sales lag. We also raise our forecasts for Weichai by 7-8% for FY16-17E, expecting a strong 3Q which should trigger upward revisions in earnings forecasts by street analysts.

  • •    Cyclical rebound taking hold: 2015 was a tough year, driven by tight credit & destocking; excavator and HDT sales recorded their largest decline in history, down c40% and c30% Y/Y respectively. Expectations at the beginning of this year were thus low, while sales through the first three quarters have run well ahead, supported by restocking and robust demand from infra investment and logistics activities. Growth momentum since July has further accelerated, driven by steady growth in infra FAI and a pickup in land sales for property investment.

  • •    On-the-ground construction activity has improved notably, as reflected by end-user utilization rates for excavators. Cash conversion cycle for end users has also improved through 2016. As a result the receivables overdue ratio for machinery makers has fallen to cl4% in 2016 from >18% oya driven, in part, by stronger regulations with respect to gov't spending on infra construction and a push for more timely payments to construction contractors. Additionally, the recent rise in PPP projects has also improved cash settlement.

  • •    Weichai remains our top pick. We raise FY16/17E by 7-8%. Our forecasts are 14%/17% ahead of consensus. In light of strong sales YTD, Weichai’s mgmt raised its full-year industry sales guidance to 660k units, implying c20% Y/Y growth (raised from flatfish growth guided earlier this year). Weichai has been regaining the lost ground since Beiqi’s cut back last year, lifting its HDT engine market share to >25% in 2016 from last year’s 21%. Based on October’s production plan, Weichai's engine sales growth may further pick up to >60% Y/Y, suggesting upside potential to our raised forecasts.

  • •    Expect strong 3Q. We forecast Weichai’s 3Q16 earnings at cRmb0.5B, up c580% Y/Y in part on a low base. We note consensus has been stale while skepticism remains high despite the accelerating growth momentum, hence we expect the upcoming strong result to drive upward revisions in forecasts
