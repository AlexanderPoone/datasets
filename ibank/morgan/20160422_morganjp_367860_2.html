Upside risks to our view:

Better than expected improvement in margins; market share gains in mid/high end products.

Downside risks to our view:

Key downside risks include: i) the overall market has seen a volume growth slowdown so market share gains become a critical driver for growth, ii) intensifying competition in beer industry might squeeze CRB's margins and drag down earnings.
