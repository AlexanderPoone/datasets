Long-term targets raised. BNEF raised their longer-term PV deployment targets, which are primarily determined by countiy specific targets rather than company pipelines and backlogs. BNEF expects Germany to announce a new target of 200 GW of cumulative PV solar by 2030, up from an existing target of 98 GW. BNEF believes the Russian invasion of the Ukraine likely presents short-term headwinds to capex for renewable projects owing to expected inflation impacts on raw materials, though over the medium/long-term, European countries are likely to accelerate their transitions to renewables and energy independence.
