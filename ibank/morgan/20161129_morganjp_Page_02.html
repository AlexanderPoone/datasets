Key catalysts forthe stock price: Noodes upgrade (from Rmb3.5to Rmb4) and re-positioning (from Rmb4.5 to Rmb5) may cause Tingyi to lose some market share in the near term and drag down its sales.

Upside risks to our view:

  • 1)    A sudden sharp improvement in beverages sales via strong product development and market share gains and similarly better-than-expected sales in noodles on the back of products upgrade;

  • 2)    A further sharp decline in input costs leading to notable gross margin expansion;

  • 3)    Pepsi margins coming through better than expected.

Downside risks to ourview:

  • (1)    Sales momentum worsening in 2H16 despite the introduction of new upgraded noode products,

  • (2)    Sudden worsening competition, leading to higher A&P

  • (3)    Steep hikes in input costs, leading to notable gross margin contraction
