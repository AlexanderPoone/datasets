Tencent

Conviction OW as both a defensive and offensive player

Tencent remains our top conviction stock in the Internet sector given its versatility' in mobile internet and its counter-cyclical business model. Its superior positioning in China's social networking market particularly in gaming and social ads, provides further assurance. We expect growth in FYI6 to further shift to mobile-based video and performance ads, and PC/mobile gaming to generate continually solid cash flow. Arguably now in a more steady growth stage, we think Tencent will retain its leadersltip in mobile gaming via genre diversification and a pan-entertainment strategy. We expect mobile P4P ads to grow at a 16-18E CAGR of 66% vs. mobile gaming at 26%. We assume coverage of Tencent with an Overweight rating and Dec-16 PT of HKS179.

  • •    Expect mobile performance ads to underpin the next phase of growth, rising 116%/73% YoY to RMB15.4bn/26.7bn in FYT6/17E, where WeChat ad revs (Moments and Public Accounts) contribute 55%/66%. We expect WeChat ad monetization, especially Moments ads, to accelerate from FY16. We estimate Moments ad revenue will surpass Mobile Qzone by FY17E (RMB10.9bn from Moments vs. RMB7.8bn from Mobile Qzone). We expect Moments ads to further reach 19% of Facebook mobile newsfeeds’ ad revenue byFY18(from 15% in FY17 and 12% inFY16).

  • •    Unshaken leadership in China’s mobile gaming market. We believe Tencent has a critical role in leading China’s mobile gaming market’s shift towards IP-driven growth. It fine-tuned its strategy in 2015 to adapt for the PC-to-mobile IP migration, as well as pushing forward genre diversification on mobile. Aimed with a deep understanding and rich experience in game development & operations, we expect Tencent to continue to take share in China's mobile gaming market (JPMe 38% in FY16E, 42% in FY17E and 46% in FY18E).

  • •    Dec-i6 SoTP-based PT of HK$i79: 1) HK$37 from PC game & SNS on 12x 2016E P/E, 2) HK$43 from mobile games on 25x 2016E P/E, 3) HK$7 9 from online ad on 53x 2016E P/E, 4) HK$ 10 from equity investments, and 5) HK$ 11 from net cash. Our PT implies 3 lx 2016E P/E and a PEG of 1.2x.
