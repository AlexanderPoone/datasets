Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

China Gas Holdings Limited (Overweight; Price Target: HK$12.30)

Investment Thesis

China Gas Holdings Limited is engaged in the construction and operation of natural gas distribution, the sale of LPG, and construction and operation of CNG/LNG stations. As of 31 Oct 2016, it had 305 city gas projects, 14.7MM residential customers and 571 CNG/LNG stations. It sold lObcm of natural gas in FY16.

Our bullish investment thesis on China Gas is based on: (1) the company’s -20% net profit growth in FY17-18E, which we believe has not been fully factored into its share price, (2) potential upside from market share gain in the LPG business, and (3) further growth in penetration.

Valuation

Our Jun-17 price target of HK$ 12.3 is based on DCF with a 7.7% WACC and a 2% terminal growth rate. Our PT implies FYI 8E P/E of 13.6x and P/B of 2.3x.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Risks to our PT include lower-than-expected gas sales and lower-than-expected new household connections.
