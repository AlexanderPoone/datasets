As such, bonds trading with a spread of lOOObp or more remains below June’s multi-year high. On a spread basis, $117.8bn or 8.6% of the HY market now trades at a spread above lOOObp ($14bn m/m) versus $149bn in June and as high as $418bn or 32.0% in March 2020. Note this metric averaged 10% of the universe in 4Q19 ahead of the pandemic. Healthcare bonds account for 22% of bonds trading 1000bp+, which is followed by Gaming/Lodging/Leisure (10%) and Technology (9%). The largest contributors of bonds trading wide of lOOObp are BHCCN, CCL, CVNA, NEWLSQ, and DSPORT. Historically, a 1000bp+ bond universe of 8-12% has produced a median and average 12M forward default rate of 1.3% and 2.4% (53 examples).
