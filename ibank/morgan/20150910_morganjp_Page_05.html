Valuation and share price analysis

DCF valuation

Our Dec-16 PT is based on a DCF valuation that assumes a market risk premium of 6.0% and a risk free rate of 4.2% (yield on 10-year government notes in China). We have assumed a beta of 1.3 (Bloomberg Beta is 1.3). Accordingly, WACC is assumed at 11.4%. We have estimated free cash flow for XYG until 2020 and assume a terminal growth rate of 3%. The terminal growth is based on the annual growth rate expected in 2020 (the final year of the estimate period) subject to a minimum of 3% and a maximum of 6% depending on the nature of the industry and the level of maturity in China.

We also analyzed the DCF price sensitivity to WACC, and the terminal multiple.
