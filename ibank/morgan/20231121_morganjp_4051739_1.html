US Equity Financing and AIR TRF Monitor

Nov 21,2023


In this publication, we provide analytics on equity financing markets and adjusted interest rate total return fritures (AIR TRFs), including charts and analytics on pricing, term structure, open interest, and volumes.


Highlights


  • •    S&P 500 implied financing rates were little changed w/w, apart from a moderate increase in the IM tenor w/w

  • •    The Nasdaq 100 and Russell 2000 funding term structures both flattened w/w given increases in the IM IFR: forthe Nasdaq 100 the restofthecurvewas little changed, while for the Russell 2000 the 3M-1Y segment of the curve also increased moderately

  • •    Pricing on S&P500 AIR TRFs was little changed w/w(chgs< 1 bp), apart from the Dec’23 contract that increased 6 bps w/w on the spot market rally

  • •    Volumes on S&P 500 AIR TRFs totaled $6.3Bn over the last week (1.4z), with the majority of trading taking place on the Dec’23, Mar’24, and Dec’24 contracts

  • •    Open interest on the S&P500 AIR TRFs increased by $6.2Bn w/w, around half of which was due to the spot rally

  • •    The Russell 2000 and Nasdaq 100 AIR TRFs did not trade over the last week
