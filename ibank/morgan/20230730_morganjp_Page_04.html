Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Trip.com Group Ltd - H (Overweight; Price Target: HK$393.00)

Investment Thesis

We turn more positive on Trip.com following the Politburo meeting that urged an increase in outbound flight supply. Our bullish view on Trip.com is based more on market share gains due to sustainable factors and less on a general outbound travel market recovery. We see structural growth opportunities in areas such as outbound travel and corporate travel, driven by accelerating market share gains, optimized supply chain and improving operating efficiency. In the short term, we believe pent-up demand offers upside risk to consensus estimates for 2Q/3Q23. In 2024, we expect market share gains in outbound travel market will lead to strong earnings growth.

Valuation

Our Jun-24 PT of HK$3 93 is based on a 20x 2024E P/E. Our target multiple represents a premium to domestic OTA peer Tongcheng (16x 2024E P/E), as we believe Trip.com as industry leader has clear competitive edges in user scale, technology and profitability, etc. Our H-share PT is adjusted from our ADR PT using an exchange rate of HK$7.8:US$ 1.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Downside risks to our rating and price target include: (1) a slower-than-expected recovery of the travel market; (2) intense industry competition; and (3) macroeconomic slowdown.
