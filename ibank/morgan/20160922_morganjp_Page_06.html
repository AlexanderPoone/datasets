We estimate tissue to have a value of HK$1.0

We have prepared a DCF valuation below for the tissue business only and value it at Dec 16 DCF based fair value of approx. HK$1.0 per share. This value is about double the book value of the investment by LMP until the end of Dec 2016 and the premium 2x P/B for the tissue business reflects the economic value of LMP’s low cost of production (through co-location with a large containerboard production business) that is not achievable by any other tissue manufacturers, in our view. The value premium over the book value also factors in some upside from the branded business that LMP is developing although the exact value of the brand is factored into the ASP and net profit per ton of the blended (OEM and branded) business. Based on our new assessment of the tissue business value, we are comfortable lifting the overall Dec-17 DCF-basedPT to HK$10.1 (previously HK$9.3). We had previously included a minor value (about HK$0.3) for the tissue business (in our previous PT) but now have more reported performance figures to estimate a higher value for this business.

Replacement value of 5.3 is a good support floor level

We highlight below the replacement value for LMP is HK$5.3 per share (as at Dec 16) which includes the replacement value of the tissue business. This is the total amount of investment that a new entrant would need to invest in order to replicate the production capabilities of NDP. We have adjusted this figure for the impact of a depreciating euro (makes equipment made in Europe cheaper) as well as factoring in the age of LMP’s machines. This is a theoretical replacement value because we think new entrants are unlikely as they would need similar licences, locations and supply chains as the incumbents and these may not be available. Furthermore, large amounts of capital required and the cyclical nature of this business increasingly discourage new entrants.

Typically, the replacement value is a defensive ‘floor value’ for a containerboard producer. In the past, the share price of LMP has exceeded the replacement value by over three times during periods. If we get to the next tight market with GPM exceeding 20%, it would not be unreasonable to have this ratio approach a 1,8x to 2.0x range, in our view.
