Dominant position in a niche market

The growing consumer demand for high-quality and immersive watching experience has led to strong demand for non-conventional theatre screens. IMAX, as the leading non-conventional theatre screen brand, is likely to be the key beneficiary from this trend.

  • •    IMAX has had a presence in China for nearly 15 years, and enjoys widespread recognition and consumer loyalty. Our discussion with theatre companies reveals that a major reason for their interests in IMAX is for branding purposes, as having IMAX differentiates oneself not only by the quality of sendee but also the premier theatre image.

  • •    IMAX has established relationships with over 30 exhibitors in China, especially the industry leader Wanda Cinema Line, which has installed IMAX theatres in the majority of its new theatres in recent years.

  • •    The IMAX theatre technology delivers a superb cinematic experience, which is widely considered to be the best in the market.

  • •    IMAX has a good track record of exhibiting a strong slate of Hollywood content complemented by a growing portfolio of Chinese films. The top 10 highest grossing films of 2015 were all released in IMAX format and the second-highest grossing Chinese language film in history was in IMAX format too.
