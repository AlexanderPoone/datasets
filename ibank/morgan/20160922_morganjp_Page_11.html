Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Lee & Man Paper Manufacturing (Overweight; Price Target: HK$10.10)

Investment Thesis

Lee & Man Paper Manufacturing is the second-largest containerboard maker in China and among the top 5 globally. Demand is driven by domestic usage, which is over 80% of end consumer demand. Improvement in the retail sales of consumable products (groceries, drink, personal care) drive demand. Rising pollution standards and the forced closure of small suppliers are improving the supply/demand equation. We expect a benign to positive pricing environment for the next one to two years.

Valuation

Our Dec-17 price target is based on a DCF valuation that assumes a market risk premium of 6.0% and a risk-free rate of 4.2% (the yield on 10-year government notes in China). We assume a beta of 1.3 (the sector’s long-term beta). Accordingly, WACC is assumed at 9.6%.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Downside risks to our rating and price target include: new supply coming online, depressing margins; a surge in the cost of old cardboard due to a booming U.S. economy; and credit tightening, crimping demand from end customers and causing box makers to destock inventories.
