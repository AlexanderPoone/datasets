Al heal dissipation solutions vs. regular server solutions.

Currently some ISPs adopt immersion liquid cooling as a transitional solution, which is costly but has better heat dissipation performance vs. air cooling We believe solution providers have been researching on a more economical liquid cooling (cold-plate) solution to tackle the much higher heat generation of Al servers (such as II100 server).




Networking: NVIlnk anti NVSwItch

It takes multiple Al servers to train a single algorithm so the data transmission between GPUs in different Al servers is important to reduce the latency. Nvidia has launched NVIink to improve the communication between GPUs within a single Al server and NVSwtich to connect the various Al rack servers. Consequently, we expect the rising Al server mix to drive the networking upgrade in datacenter switch and volume upsides of Smart NIC DPU.
