Global Outlook: Rising financial stability risks but recession does not appear imminent

Setting up guardrails

In contrast to last year’s fall and spring IMF/World Bank meetings, last week’s investor seminar during the 2023 Spring IMF/World Bank meetings were down beat but also not overly bearish, in our view. There were few signs of complacency with “fragmentation,” “setting up guardrails” and “re-globalization, not decoupling” among the key buzzwords and catch phrases. Our investor survey indicates that investors are braced for greater downside.
