Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Industrial and Commercial Bank of China - H (Overweight; Price

Target: HK$6.15)

Investment Thesis

  • •    Strong capital position: ICBC’s core tier 1 ratio is one of the highest among peers, due to its prudent strategy and low exposure to non-standardized assets.

  • •    Cost management continues to improve - opex went up only modestly and the cost-to-income ratio continues to trend down.

  • •    NPL coverage ratio dropped below 150%, below regulatory minimum of 150%, which implies the relaxation of NPL coverage by regulators will provide limited benefits to ICBC.

  • •    Interbank liabilities only accounted for mid teens of IBL, one of the lowest among peers.

Valuation

Our Dec-17 price target of HK$6.15 is based on DDM using a cost of equity of

  • 12.7%, normalised ROE of 11.2% and terminal date of 31 December 2018.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Key risks to our PT are: lower-than-expected fee growth; deterioration in pricing power, which could lead to NIM erosion; and/or a sharp rise in non-performing loans, which could push up credit costs.
