Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Link REIT (Overweight; Price Target; HKS56.40)

Investment Thesis

While Link REIT’s expansion into China office/retail and Hong Kong office has raised a lot of concerns, we believe the majority of its business and earnings still come from the defensive non-discretionary retail malls in Hong Kong, which should be able to offset the negative impact from its China expansion strategy, in our view. Also, the company is one of the few HK property companies which has consistently been trying to add value and actively manage its capital, i.e., selling mature assets in HK and buying back its own units.

Valuation

Our Dec-16 price target of HK$56.4 is based on a 4% premium to its latest book value, which is 0.5 S.D. below its average premium. The PT is also in line with the Dec-16 NPV derived from a terminal growth assumption of 1.15%.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Downside risks to our price target and rating include a sudden rise in interest rates/ long bond yields, higher-than-expected vacancy, lower-than-expected rental reversions, a delay in the completion of AEIs and a sharp slowdown in the Hong Kong and China economy.
