Key Currency Views

Fragility unveiled

  • •    The market is flitting from theme to theme, unveiling underlying fragility.

  • •    The near-term dollar view remains contingent on event risk and growth outturns. USD can resume weakening if US terminal rates stabilize; but a 50bp would be regime-shift.

  • •    Additional central bank hikes are raising medium-term concerns around hard landing and resulting USD strength. We discuss FX behavior around onset of recessions.

  • •    Main trading themes are—long euro on crosses like SEK pre-ECB, long JPY on crosses on US recession hedges and eventual BoJ pivot and global FX carry. Within GIO. the high beta FX like Scandis, NZD and CAD remain our preferred underweights.

  • •    FX Targets subject to revision on event risk: USD JPY lowered to reflect delayed BoJ, hakwish Fed; 4Q 134 (128), 1Q’24 133. AUD/USD lowered 3Q 0.68 (0.70) on Fed. EUR USD unchanged 2Q 1.10. 1Q24 1.08.

  • •    In EM, USD ON Y 3Q 6.90 (6.75), 1Q’24 6.85 on hawkish Fed. USDBRL 3Q 5.20 (unchanged). USDMXN 3Q 19.25 (unchanged).
