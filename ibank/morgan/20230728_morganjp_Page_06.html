Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Hyundai Mobis (Overweight; Price Target: W290,000)

Investment Thesis

Hyundai Mobis’ 2Q23 OP (W664B) came largely in line with our and Street expectations, with cost stabilization driving gradual margin improvement. While revenue grew solidly across all divisions, we are especially encouraged to see its A/S business continuously growing from last year’s high base. On the profit side, we are seeing cost deflation gradually kicking in at both the A/S and module businesses, which will likely continue throughout 2H23. Electrification revenue expands at a rapid pace, but OP remains slightly negative on ongoing investments. We note, however, that upon rapid revenue expansion, the R&D % of Electrification/Core parts revenue is gradually dissipating, an indication that R&D is now being well covered with scale. We expect a gradual turnaround in Electrification profits as HMC/Kia EV ramps up further. We maintain estimates broadly unchanged, with our PT raised slightly, to W290k (rolled to Jun-24); OW.

Valuation

Our Jun-24 PT of W290k is based on a target PZE of 8x, a slight discount to the peer average due to expected near-term volatility.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Key downside risks to our rating and price target include: (1) production disruptions from chip shortages; (2) FX; and (3) extended margin pressure from the Electrification business.
