  • •    Oil-focused operators dropped by two to 590 rigs, marking five consecutive weekly drops.

  • •    Natural gas-focused rigs dropped one to 153, with core gas regions yet to see significant drops.

  • •    The rig count in the five major tight oil basins—we use the EIAbasin definition —dropped by five to 559 rigs.

  • •    Since our last publication, the EIA reported that US crude and oil condensate production averaged 12.20 mbd in December. The reported production was 100 kbd below our monthly estimate, where we assumed that winter storm Elliott caused production to be curtailed by 255 kbd. The additional miss can be attributed to the shut-ins, as many operators reported impacts to their top-line 4Q production due to the storm.
