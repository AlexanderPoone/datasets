Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Hutchison Telecom HK (Neutral; Price Target: HKS2.75)

Investment Thesis

HTHKH is seeing pressure in both mobile and fixed-line businesses. Besides a potential decline in handset sales and slower momentum of high-end plan adoptions, HTHKH’s mobile business is also facing more challenges than peers due to its relatively high exposure to roaming service and minimal exposure to the low-end segment which is benefiting from upward price movements. Its fixed-line operations, which used to provide a good hedge against the more volatile mobile business are losing steam due to on-going terminations of CSL’s backhaul leasing contracts, corporate’s declining IDD sendee demand and a pick-up in residential broadband competition. However, HTHKH benefits from a favorable cost structure with integrated asset base, the improving mobile trend with recent increase in SIM-only plan charges a positive and high yield.

Valuation

Our Dec-16 PT of HK$2.75 is based on our SOTP valuation comprising HK$1.3 for the mobile operation and HK$1.5 for fixed-line and others. We use a DCF approach to value both the mobile and fixed-line businesses and combine the two to arrive at our price target.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Downside risks to our rating and price target include increase in mobile and broadband competition and prolonged sluggish growth in fixed-line business. Upside risks include further mobile price increase this year and stronger-than-expected fixed-line demand from carriers and corporates.
