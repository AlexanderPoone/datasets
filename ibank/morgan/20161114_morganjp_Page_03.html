Recent developments

Creation of investment fund

China Gas plans to invest Rmb3B into an investment fund to acquire gas projects. The fund’s partner, China Insurance Investment Fund (CIIF), would invest Rmb7B. CIIF can seek early redemption of 20% of its capital contribution upon the fifth anniversary of its first contribution, and 60% upon the seventh anniversary. China Gas would be the general partner of the fund and would take a 70% stake in the GP.

JPM comments. The establishment of this fund would give China Gas access to off-balance sheet funding. We view this as a positive from the funding point of view, but would like to see disclosures in the future on the types of investments this fund would make and the valuation paid for the assets.

Asset injection from parent co further delayed

China Gas has further delayed the completion of the asset injection from parent co Beijing Enterprises Holdings to 31 Dec 2016, from 30 Sept, due to additional time needed to secure the required government approvals. In June 2016, China Gas revised its acquisition plans for BEH’s 12 city gas projects. It would first acquire 10 of those projects and the midstream assets and acquire the two remaining city gas projects from Hebei Tangshan at a later date. We have not factored in the acquisition into our FY17E EPS.
