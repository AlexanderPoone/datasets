Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Coca-Cola Icecek AS (Overweight; Price Target: TL77.00)

Investment Thesis

  • •    Winner of opening economy with high margin on premise share in revenues is high at 20% in Turkey, 15% in Pakistan

  • •    Robust profitability triggered, by top class cost execution and lean SKU management remain stable next year

  • •    Deep discount to global bottlers trading at 48% discount on trailing EBITDA multiples

Valuation

We updated our forecast model for CCOLA reflecting stronger than expected profitability which we expect to be maintained in 2021. In the meantime, we roll over our cash flow driven PT to end 2021 and raise our PT to TL77. Our main assumptions for the DCF valuation include a WACC of 17.2% and a long-term growth rate of 7%.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

The main downside risk could come from third wave of COVID-19 virus and hence further restrictions in on-trade premises as on-trade consumption is important to sustain volume growth and margins at higher level. Other risks include intensifying competition in core markets (Pakistan), and currency risks in Turkey and CCI’s international markets such as Pakistan. The main operational risk, in our view, is that populations in the Middle East turn against American brands, which would clearly be negative for the Coca-Cola brand, in particular.
