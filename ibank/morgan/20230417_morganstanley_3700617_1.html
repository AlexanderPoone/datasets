Global FX Positioning: EUR

Complacency


In the week ending April 14, options pricing data indicate that investors added to long CAD and CHF positionsand increased their short NZDand JPY positions. In the futures market, USD shorts were added against GBP and CAD, while investors bought USD against AUD in the week ending Tuesday, April 11 Overall, investors continue to be long EUR in both the optionsand futures markets. Contrary to what options data suggest, CAD and CHF positioning remain short in the futures market. Options and futures data indicate short USD (DXY) positions. However, the magnitude of this position has not returned to the stretched levels observed in January.
