BYD Company Limited - H

Another beat


Overweight

1211.HK, 1211 HK

Price: HK$51.40

Price Target: HK$75.00



BYD’s 3Q16 result was a moderate beat vs. our/ consensus estimates and at the higher end of guidance. We raise our 2016E earnings by 7% to reflect the beat. We believe the company’s motor battery capacity is one of its most valuable assets while fast-growing xEV demand in the China market would continue to boost BYD’s sales in both passenger car (EV and PHEV) and EV bus businesses. Besides policy regulation supporting leading motor battery suppliers (like BYD), NDRC’s recent announcement that China will impose a carbon emission trading scheme from 2017 (click here) should further mitigate potential earnings shortfall from reduced financial subsidy. BYD-H is among our top picks in the China auto OEM space besides Geely and Brilliance China.

• Recap of our thesis/future catalysts: 1) We are bullish on China's NEV industry and believe this will be a powerful, multi-year structure trend (30% CAGR towards 2020). 2) China government recently announced it will impose a carbon trade scheme and is expected to announce its investigation result on NEV fraud sales soon. We view these as structurally positive for leading OEMs such as BYD. 3) We believe industry battery supply will remain tight in the near term. Our view is that the value of BYD lies primarily in its battery capability and capacity.
