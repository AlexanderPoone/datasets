Large High Yield Bond Funds Outflows Recently

High yield markets have seen large outflows on a weekly basis since mid November - there have been net outflows of $ 16.8 bil cumulatively. There have been four weeks of inflows: during the firstand last week of December, the last week of January, and a tad last week; but these inflows were small at $1.5bil cumulatively. The large outflows have added significant pressure to spreads in the high yield markets. This outflow follows a reversal of large inflows into high yield bond funds over prior couple of years as investors sought out higher yields in the face of low rates.
