XYG should have sufficient export sales to offset non-Rmb denom mated costs.

  • •    We do not expect X YG to report an FX translation loss on its HK dollar debt because the functional currency of the company is the HK dollar.

Aside from FX issues, we did revise down our expectations of a price recovery of float glass in 2016 from 10% to 5% after seeing the disappointing margins for float glass (plain float glass at negative GPM) in rival China Glass interim results.

As a result the combined FX and lower float glass prices, we lower net profits for FY15 and FY16 by 7.1 % and 13.8% respectively

Our revised FY16 EPS estimate is 5% lower than consensus estimates for FY16 but we highlight that many estimates of consensus may not have factored in the latest changes in FX assumptions. We lowered our DCF based Dec 16 PT to HK$5.2 (prev. HK$5.5).
