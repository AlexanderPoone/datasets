Asia Pacific Equity Research 18 April 2023



^—Online physical goods GM V monthly YoY


  • • Online apparel sales growth has showed clear acceleration. According to NBS, apparel online sales in 1Q23 increased by 8.6% YoY vs. 4% in 2M23, suggesting high teens or even 20% plus YoY growth in March, based on our calculation. Daily-use products’ online sales growth improved gradually at 6.9% YoY in 1Q23 vs. 5.7% in 2M23. Food online sales growth in 1Q23 also accelerated to 7.3% YoY in 1Q23 from 5.3% in 2M23. The strong online apparel sales favor Alibaba/Vipshop the most.
