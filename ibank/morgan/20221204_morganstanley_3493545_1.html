Focus List Changes - APxJ, GEM, and China/HK

We switch into ANTA Sports across our APxJ, GEM, and China/HK Focus Lists from DBS Group, Enel Chile, and Swire Pacific, respectively. In our APxJ Focus List and GEM Focus List, we also switch into SEMCO, removing LG Display given greater price target upside and longer-term return prospects.

Here we provide a brief summary. Please see our Investment Thesisfor Focus List Additions in the following section for more detail.

Adding ANTA Sports Products (2020.HK) to the APxJ, GEM, and China/HK

Focus Lists: Morgan Stanley analyst Dustin Wei thinks ANTA is a good reopening play, coupled with a compelling Long-term investment thesis. Dustin and his team view ANTA as one of the fastest-growing consumer companies and believe that the coming macro up-cycle and easing in COVID should drive re-rating.

® See ANTA Sports: Low Expectations + Constructive Guidance = Stock Performance, August 23, 2022.

We make room for ANTA by removing the following:

® APxJ Focus List - DBS Group Holdings (DBSM.SI)

® GEM Focus List - Enel Chile (ENELCHILE.SN)

® China/HK Focus List - Swire Pacific (0019.HK)

Switching into Samsung Electro-Mechanics (009150.KS) from LG Display (034220.KS) in the APxJ and GEM Focus Lists: Morgan Stanley's head of Asia Technology research, Shawn Kim, recently double-upgraded SEMCO to Overweight. Shawn and his team believe that the stock is at an attractive entry point with a compelling risk-reward skew. The team had been long been cautious on SEMCO, but in its view, estimates have now been cut sufficiently. The team believes that the cycle is closer to an inflection (bottom) and the stock has derated enough for them to see it in a much more favorable Light.

® See Samsung Electro-Mechanics: Looking Through to 2023: Risk-Reward Turns Positive - Double Upgrade to Overweight, November 8, 2022.
