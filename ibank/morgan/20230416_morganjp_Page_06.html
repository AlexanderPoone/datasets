More complete Al ecosystem attracts new investment

Al is playing a critical role in the world. There are more use cases relying on Al algorithms and machine learning to save time and costs across industries. People are more frequently using ChatGPT to collect data and make better decisions in a shorter time frame. Businesses arc leveraging Al algorithms to automate operational processes to improve efficiency and save costs. According to a McKinsey & Company survey. Al adoption in enterprises has more than doubled in 2022 (50%) vs. 2017 (20%).

The rise of underlying Al demand will lead to a more complete Al ecosystem, in our view. During Nvidia’s GTC investor day. the company indicated strong growth in the number of developers. CUDA downloads. Al startups, and GPU-accelerated applications. Similarly. Intel saw 85%+ install base increase in its open accelerated computing platform during the investor webinar. Given that server capacity and computing power arc the key factors of Al infrastructure, we believe the pent-up Al demand and emerging Al applications will lead to a fast growth of Al server spending. According to IDC. Al server infrastructure will grow at a 17% CAGR in 2021-26.
