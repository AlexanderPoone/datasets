Raising excavator sales forecasts

Excavator forecasts for FY16/17 raised to +11%/+6% in light of stronger-than-expected growth YTD while noting strong rebound in domestic demand in recent months. Recovery in infrastructure FAI spending in recent months has supported the rebound in excavator sales with large-sized excavators in particular benefitting from increased activity on projects in South-Western China.
