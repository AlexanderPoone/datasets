Fufeng Group

New PT HK$6.5 - core business doing well but many other headwinds in 2015

Fufeng is the largest producer of Monosodium Glutamate (MSG) in China with over a 60% market share. Fufeng reported disappointing interim results, due mainly to weakness in the Xanthum gum business, but the core MSG business is improving and we maintain OW - new Dec 16 PT of HK$6.5.

  • •    Focus on the core business. We believe the share price performance over the past three months reflects a deterioration of the Xanthum Gum business as well as concerns over USD debts. However, we continue to highlight steady improvements in the core MSG operations, with GPM significantly higher than the troughs in 2012-13. We expect the core business to continue to improve in 2016 and believe additional growth will come in the new high-end amino acids (sales up over 60% in 1H Y/Y) as Fufeng is a cost leader and moving up the value chain in more advanced products with higher ASP. We see current concerns over the Xanthum Gum division and one-off losses related to the debts as a buying opportunity for this global leader in fermented products.

  • •    Lowering estimates and setting new PT of HKS6.5. We maintain our sales estimate for 2015 and lift 2016 sales to factor in higher MSG prices. The reduction in EBIT in 2015 is mainly due to the weaker-than-expected performance in Xanthum Gum in 1H15 which should carry on into 2H15. EBIT is lowered to factor in the lower GPM as well as higher GA costs. We added an extra loss in 2015 to reflect the impact of the depreciating Renminbi on USD denominated debt. Our new Dec 15 PT is HK$6.5 (prev. Dec 15 PT of HK$7.7).
