Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Chow Tai Fook Jewellery Company Ltd. (Underweight; Price Target: HKS3.50)

Investment Thesis

CTF’s share price has been weak over the past two years (fell 53% vs HSI down 10%) largely because of weak HK while China is relatively resilient. A sudden deterioration in China’s business in 4QFY16 may fuel market concerns if it further pressures weak HK business. We don’t see any positive catalysts in the near term. Maintain UW.

Valuation

Our Mar-17 PT is HK$3.5, based on lOx one year forward PE which is 2 stdev below historical mean. Our PT cut is due to earnings revision.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

The main downside risks to our rating and price target include the HK sales decline continuing for longer than expected, an inability to keep the cost-plus model, FX impact and unexpected pressure on margins from promotions and costs.

Upside risks include better-than-expected sales growth in gem-set sales, less pronounced normalization of gold sales and higher-than-expected margin improvements from a shift in mix to non-gold products.
