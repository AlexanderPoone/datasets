Summary Investment Thesis and Valuation

Despite macro uncertainties, which in our view have already been priced into investor expectations, we think Alibaba's cost optimization initiatives will continue to drive profit upside in the coming quarters. We believe the bottom-line growth rate bottomed out in June quarter and will accelerate to 35%+ YoY in March quarter 2023, driven by 1) a recovery of highly profitable domestic ecommerce revenue (CMR), and 2) cost optimization across a large number of operations. We expect earnings revision to turn positive post June quarter and to serve as a k⑶ catalyst in the next few quarters.
