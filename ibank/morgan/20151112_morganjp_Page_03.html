Earnings and DPU changes

We revised up our revenue and NPI estimates for FY16-18 modestly to reflect the higher car park rentals and better NPI margin. We also adjusted down the interest expense as Link secured the new financing of HK$ 10.4 bn at Hibor + 1.02% to 1.23%. This led to a roughly 3% upward revision of DPU. The Dec-16NPV was raised by 7.0%, mainly coming from the DPU increase.
