Stronger GM a recurring luxury theme

As an aside, we note that stronger GM has overall been a recurring theme in luxury reporting in Hl 15 helped by forex and stronger sales at full price, and despite a well flagged negative geo mix. This is surprisingly not yet putting to rest the bears’ argument that GM dynamics for the industry are over. In our view, the sector has enough challenges to face as is that it is not needed or fair to overlook one of its key positives.


H1 operating expenses were in line +17% yoy, with Q2 opex tailing off albeit still high expected (+11% vs. Q1 +22%), helped by front loading of A&P, albeit still high (+24% in Q2 vs. +32% in Q1) and less forex impact.

H1 15 Net Income was down 23% to €189m.
