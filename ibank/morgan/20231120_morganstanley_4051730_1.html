2024 Asia Macro Strategy Outlook I Asia Pacific

Three Themes into 2024

We position for three themes heading into 2024:1) A sub-par China recovery not translating into immediate CNY weakness; 2) Long carry in Asia FX; and 3) Add bullish duration trades selectively.

We see three key themes driving AXJ FX and rates markets as we head into 2024:

  • 1)    A sub-par China recovery not translating into immediate CNY weakness: The stable or even lower USD/CNY fixing has prevented RMB from weakening, but we believe that a weaker RMB is ultimately warranted given the worsening balance of payments and China's sub-par growth recovery. We suggest short CNH versus IDR and TWD, and long 6m USD/CNH straddle.

  • 2)    Long carry in Asia FX: For now, a stable CNY could help to provide stability for the broader Asian markets, supporting some carry trades in the next month or two. We like long USD/MYR as a medium-term long USD and Long carry trade, in addition to short CNH/IDR.

  • 3)    Add bullish duration trades selectively: Investors may pause their duration reduction into year-end or even chase the year-end duration rally should US data remain soft. Markets pricing in an earlier Fed rate cut may spill over to Asia too. We Like receiving 5-year CNY NDIRS, 1-year INR NDOIS and 2-year KRW NDIRS versus 2-year USD.

In FX, we recommend:

  • •    Short CNH/IDR 3m NDF

  • •    Short CNH/TWD

  • •    Long USD/MYR 3m NDF

  • •    Long 6m USD/CNH straddle
