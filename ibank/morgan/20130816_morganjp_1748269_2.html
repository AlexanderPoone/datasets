Company Description

Agile Property (3383.HK) is one of the leading homebuilders in China. Based in Guangdong, the Group focuses on large-scale residential community developments and is ranked one of the top real estate developers in Guangdong Province in terms of sales. As of March 2013, Agile has total landbank of 35 million sqm in Guangzhou, Zhongshan and other Guangdong cities, as well as Nanjing, Chengdu, Shenyang, Xi’an, Changzhou. Agile also has certain investment property portfolio in Shanghai, Sanya and Guangdong Province.

Price target and valuation analysis

Our Jun-14 PT is based on 6.0x FY13/14E average P/E. Our target P/E is lower than the average of mid-caps as we think Agile's sales are likely to underperform its peers in the near term.
