Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Haci Omer Sabanci Holding A.S. (Overweight; Price Target: TL15.20)

Investment Thesis

  • •    Irrational NAV discount (43% on current listed NAV) makes the stock a value play. Improvement of the visibility on the underlying portfolio mainly from realization via IPOs/divestments/acquisitions as well as easing concerns in Turkish banks could help narrow the current NAV in the medium term.

  • •    Capital allocation strategy that targets disciplined use of capital in order to achieve ROIOWACC +1-3% provides some comfort to us that Sabanci is likely to focus on improving return through redefining its portfolio with spin-offs and acquisitions..

  • •    Relatively less diversified NAV is a negative. Akbank, on its own generates >76% of Sabanci’s consolidated net income and its share in NAV is large at c.38%. Holding generates c.70% of its NAV from two sectors, including finance and energy.

Valuation

We value Sabanci Holding based on Target NAV and apply a discretionary 30% conglomerate discount to arrive our Dec-21 PT. We value portfolio companies in Sabanci Holding using a mix of consensus & peer multiples and a hurdle rate based valuation.
