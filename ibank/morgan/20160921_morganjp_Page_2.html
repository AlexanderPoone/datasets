Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Bank Of Communications - H (Underweight; Price Target: HK$5.50)

Investment Thesis

  • •    As the only H-share bank headquartered in the eastern coast of China (Shanghai), BoCom’s loan portfolio is more exposed to the asset quality risk of SMEs and exporters in this region.

  • •    BoCom has the weakest retail franchise among SOE banks, so it relies more on interbank liquidity to fund its growth. We believe BoCom is more vulnerable to interbank rate volatility.

  • •    BoCom’s LDR remained one of the highest among peers, and we are cautious that its balance sheet liquidity remains weak.

  • •    BoCom has one of the lowest ROE among H-share banks we cover. Thus, it has less strength to withstand headwinds from an asset cycle.

  • •    BoCom follows the same regulatory measure as SOE banks (i.e., minimum tier 1 ratio of 9.5% vs. other banks of 8.5%), but its profitability and balance sheet liquidity are significantly trailing other those of SOE banks.

Valuation

Our Dec-17 price target of HK$5.50 is DDM-derived, assuming a cost of equity of

  • 13.1 %, normalized ROE of 9.2%, and a terminal date of 31 December 2018.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Key upside risks to our rating and price target include better-than-expected macro growth - this could enable BoCom to improve its loan pricing power, which might lead to a higher ROE than our estimate.
