Key catalysts for the stock price:

  • •    Monthly new contract wins continue to beat budget

  • •    Continual margin expansion in 1H14

  • •    Better credit conditions for the local govt’s customers in China

Upside risks to our view:

  • •    Stronger growth in new projects compared to company targets

  • •    Entry into new construction sectors such as subways and other high end infrastructure works

  • •    More large project wins in Macau (we currently don’t factor in much)

Downside risks to our view:

  • •    Falling construction activity in China especially in affordable housing.

  • •    Lower than expected returns on long term projects in China

  • •    Late collections for government relate receivables
