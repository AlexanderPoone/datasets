Global Macro Strategy

We caution against selling much global sovereign bond duration, especially now that the agency MBS-related duration event is Largely in-the-price and markets price sequential ECB and BoE rate hikes into the summertime. Instead, buy duration in the US and prepare to buy duration elsewhere. We also address feedback on our view to buy USD.

Interest Rate Strategy

We maintain UST2s30s steepeners, 5s30s steepeners, and Long Sy UST outright. We close our May 133.5/134.50 Bund put spread. We maintain our July 95.625/95.875/96.125 Euribor put fly. We keep our short Bobl ASW and 2s10s ASW compression box, as well as our condifionaL 6mth forward 2s10s flatteners, our OAT 5s20s50s fly, and our EUR 30s50s swap receivers. We keep our Long OAT Nov 32 vs Bund Aug 32. We keep our paid 2y SONIA position. We maintain JGB 15s30s steepener, JPY OIS 10s30s steepener, and Long 20yJGB ASW vs ESTR.

Currency & Foreign Exchange

We maintain a bullish USD view versus EUR, AUD, and CAD as markets remain complacent to the risk of a 'hard Landing1 repricing. The Riksbank may be increasingly intolerant of SEK weakness if rates approach the effective upper bound. We discuss signals from volatility risk premia and the implied volatility term structure, and why these reinforce our short USD/JPY trade recommendation. While USD/JPY hit our stop instantaneously, we remain short USD/JPY position. We also discuss the possible reaction of USD/JPY on upcoming April MPM. We explore how the output gap should shape expectations of rate cuts from the BoC.
