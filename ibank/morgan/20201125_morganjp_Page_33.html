Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Ulker Biskuvi Sanayi AS (Neutral; Price Target: TL28.50)

Investment thesis

Ûlker is a leading Turkish branded manufacturer and a pure-play EM-focused asset with significant exposure to Turkey and MENA. Shares are value trap, trading at 5.4x 21E EV/EBITDA and trades at a large discount to European food producers’ 1 l-17x multiple. However, given its deteriorated corporate governance credentials which we think overshadow its positive earnings momentum, the valuation gap to global food producers could remain for some time. We downgrade to N.

Valuation

We roll-over our cash flow to end 2021 and cut our price target to TL28.5, largely on the back of 10% discount we apply for corporate governance credentials. To discount cash flows, we use a WACC of 17.0% and terminal growth rate of 6%.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Downside/upside risks include: 1) lower/higher than flc vols, revenues and EBITDA margin particularly in MENA region, 2) FX risk and 3) market share deterioration/appreciation. In the meantime, investors are usually concerned about corporate governance risks due to high leverage in the parent company
