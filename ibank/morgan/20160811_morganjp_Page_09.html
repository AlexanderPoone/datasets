Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

The Wharf (Holdings) Limited (Overweight; Price Target: HK$59.00)

Investment Thesis

We do not think the market will be too excited about the shares in the short term given that the run-up of the share price appears to have factored in most of the improvement. We believe the medium-term positive is the improving trend of retail sales in June and July and the numerous mall openings in 2017. Upcoming news about the potential restructuring of its telecom assets will be the next catalyst for the stock, in our view.

Valuation

Our Dec-16 price target of HK$59.0 is based on a 37% discount to NAV, which is
