Company analysis


Source: Company data.


IMAX China is the exclusive IMAX theatre system provider to film exhibitors, the exclusive licensee of the IMAX brand and the sole commercial platform for the release of IMAX format films in Greater China. It has presence in the PRC, HK and China, with 93% of IMAX film box office generated in the PRC in 2015, and the proportion still rising.

The number of IMAX theatres in Greater China has grown rapidly at a CAGR of 34% in 2012-15 to 307 theatres by 2015YE. Demand remains strong with another 100 installations expected by the company in 2016 and a total of 215 in backlog as of 2015 YE. Almost all the growth comes from the PRC market, where the number of IMAX theatres has growth at a CAGR of 42% in 2012-15 to a total of 278 by 2015 YE.
