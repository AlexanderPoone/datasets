Key catalysts for the stock price:

  • •    Quarterly earnings beat

  • •    Winning tenders in major provinces for key drugs and sales ramp up new products

  • •    Approval and launch of human insulin

  • •    Major M&A in the hospital services sector

Upside risks to our view:

  • •    Unexpected early approval of human insulin

  • •    Better penetration in China market of Ama products

  • •    Hospital services acquisition yields more synergies than expected

  • •    Much better than expected sales performance from products due to tenders.

Downside risks to our view:

  • •    Unfavorable government policies on prices of healthcare products and services.

  • •    Failure to gain from any future M&A in terms of yield synergies and growth.

  • •    Difficultly in exiting minority investments due to unfavorable market conditions.
