PCB: Higher layer counts and lower yields drive ASP upticks

We estimate 10-12 layers of regular server PCB while Al servers require higher end PCB including 18-20 layers. The higher number of PCB layers implies not only more content value but also higher difficulty in production yields. Consequently, we estimate 50-100% ASP upticks of Al server PCB vs. regular server PCB.

ODM: More complex design and system integration

While the general ODM feedback suggests similar margin for Al servers, we believe ODM margins should be diluted by the higher (¡PU costs. Still, the more complex configuration design, longer testing time, and pricing premiums for niche models will likely drive higher profit dollars.

We believe several server ODMs arc considering to change the pricing model from "buy and sell" to "consign" in Al servers. In this case, the Al server price could be reduced by 60-70% w hile margins could be higher.
