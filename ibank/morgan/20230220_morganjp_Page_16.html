Nielsen Data

The Top 10 lists are based on minutes viewed in a specific timeframe & we note Nielsen data is delayed by ~1 month. See Figures 36-39 below for Nielsen’s US Top Ten lists for the period 1/16-1/22. As indicated below, NFLX captured 24 of these top 40 spots, including 7 of the top 10 overall.
