Key catalysts for the stock price:

  • •    Growth recovery for HDTs in 2016 driven by replacement demand

  • •    Promising growth outlook for LNG trucks in the coming years

Upside risks to our view:

  • •    Stronger-than-expected recovery in heavy-duty truck sales and construction machinery sales

  • •    Stronger-than-expected market share gains

  • •    Better-than-expected synergy from the Linde acquisition and larger-than-expected investment income from KION

Downside risks to our view:

  • •    Weaker-than-expected recovery in heavy-duty truck sales and construction machinery sales

  • •    Successful vertical integration by end customers

  • •    Worse-than-expected synergy from the Linde acquisition
