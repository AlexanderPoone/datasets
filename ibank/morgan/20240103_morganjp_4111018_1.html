1. What is the governments policy framework? Growth-friendly policy will continue in 2024, but large scale stimulus is unlikely due to multi-dimensional discrepancies in assessments frompolicymakers vs. investors.


  • 2.    How will fiscal and monetary policy coordinate? The divergence between contractionary fiscal policy and accommodative monetary policy seen in2023 will not be repeated in 2024, as fiscal policy will turn expansionary. Monetary policy will support fiscal operation via liquidity management and expanded PSL and relending.

  • 3.    Will t he lion si ng market stabilize in 2024? The housing market coll ection will likely continue, as both demand and supply-side challenges persist. Public housing is important to mitigate the housing drag.

  • 5.    What will drive consumption? Income growth, adjustment in household saving rate, and whether the government will introduce consumption stimulus.

  • 6.    Will deflation end in 2024? Deflation will end in 2024, but low inflation will stay.

  • 8.    How will external conditions evolve? We expect exports will stabilize, portfolio outflows will moderate and CNY exchange rate will show two-sided volatility.

  • 9.    W ill China’s role in global supply chains change? The focus has shifted from “just-in-time” to “just-in-case” considerations. China will remain an important manufacturing hub but its dominant role will weaken.
