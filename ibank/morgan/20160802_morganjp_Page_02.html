Key catalyst for the stock price:

• Delivery of stable core earnings growth post-restructuring which will demonstrate benefits of streamlined operations

Upside risks to our view:

•Tariff hikes

  • •    Stronger-than-expected volume growth

  • •    Faster-than-expected ramp-up of port utilization

  • •    Materialization of positive synergies from COSCO/China Shipping merger

  • •    Potential EPS accretive acquisitions

  • •    Strong improvement of profitability at newly injected assets

  • •    Revaluation potential for the port assets CP currently holds should China set up more free trade zones

Downside risks to ourview:

  • •    Tariff cuts

  • •    Weaker-than-expected volume growth

  • •    Execution risk associated with new port investments
