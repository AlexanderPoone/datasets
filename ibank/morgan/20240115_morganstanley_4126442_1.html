Global FX Positioning: CHF Positioning Moves from Long to Neutral


In the week ending Friday, January 12, options pricing data indicate that investors added Long USD (DXY) and JPY positions, and increased short CHF and SEK (vs EUR) positions. In the futures market, most USD shorts were added against CAD and AUD, while investors bought USD against GBP in the week ending Tuesday, January 9, Exhibit 4 . Overall, options data suggest that tactical investors are most long NOK (versus EUR) and GBP, while being short USD (DXY). Positioning in the futures market remains long EUR and is most short AUD and USD (DXY).
