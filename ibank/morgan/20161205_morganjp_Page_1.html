• Estimate & PT changes. We update our model to reflect higher industty demand assumptions (see our note last week on November GGR). Also, given improved industty demand prospects & earnings visibility, we now assign holding-co discount of 25% to our SOTP (Table 1) vs. 33% previously; this leads us to raise our TP to HK$13.0 (old: HK$11.5), suggesting 7% potential upside to the current price.


• Recap on our sector view. We are still bullish despite the rally and recent headline noise (see our note). We believe the industry has entered a genuine upturn in demand, profits and cash-flows, which in turn should drive sustainable earnings upgrades and allow investors to enjoy predictable returns. Valuation multiples look reasonable to us at an average 13x FY17E EV/EBITDA (or llx FY18E), considering that we expect consensus upgrades and strong demand momentum, while the sector’s robust FCF yields (over 8% on average for FY17) also give us comfort. Against this backdrop, we wouldn’t tty to time a “better entry point”; it’s time to make a "directional call for longterm investment, in our view. See our in-depth report, “Time to Invest into Industry Upturn for Years Ahead” (dated 15 November 2016), for more details.
