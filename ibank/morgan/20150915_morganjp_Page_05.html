J.P. Morgan changes to estimates and target price

We make broadly no changes to estimates as the beat in Q2 GM and the continued expected GM improvement in H2 offset our lowered expectations for trading and forex help as well as higher opex in H2.

We roll forward our price target a year to Jan-17 and establish a PT of €43.
