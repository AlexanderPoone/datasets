Key catalysts for the stock price:

  • •    Market share gains for both wheel loaders and forklifts

  • •    Greater-than-expected margin improvement, driven by continued cost control

Upside risks to our view:

  • •    Stronger-than-expected recovery in wheel loader sales and excavator sales

  • •    Better-than-expected overseas expansions

  • •    Better-than-expected receivable collections

  • •    Stronger-than-expected market share gains

Downside risks to our view:

  • •    Weaker-than-expected recovery in wheel loader sales and excavator sales

  • •    Worse-than-expected receivable collections

  • •    Faster-than-expected market share losses
