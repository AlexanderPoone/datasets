Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Sun run Inc. (Overweight; Price Target; $56.00)

Investment Thesis

RUN is a leader in residential energy sen-ices including solar, storage, EV charging, home energy management, etc., an underpenetrated market that we expect to grow at a double-digit CAGR for the foreseeable future. The company has strong visibility into future revenue owing to long-term customer contracts, value per customer should improve as more sen-ices are adopted, and we believe RUN is positioned for market share gains owing to favorable ITC rules as well as the company’s leading scale.

Valuation

Reiterate Overweight. Our December 2022 price target is $56, based on an EV/cash flow multiple of 20x our FY23 cash flow estimate. Our cash flow estimate mcludes proceeds from project-level debt and tax equity in our calculation since this is directly tied to operating assets; we do not include any proceeds from corporate-level financing. Select SMid-cap software companies as well as subscription service companies with recurring cash flow streams trade between 18x and 28x EV/FCFF. We believe the stock is undervalued and expect it to outperform the mean of our coverage over the next 6-12 months

Risks to Rating and Price Target

CO VID-19 risk

The COVID-19 emergency could last longer than currently anticipated, disrupting demand and supply relative to current expectations.

Market risk

Residential solar market growth could slow relative to expectations or shift toward homeowners making outright cash purchases that bypass the lease, PPA, and loan options.
