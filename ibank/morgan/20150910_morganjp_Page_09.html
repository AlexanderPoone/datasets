Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Xinyi Glass (Overweight, Price Target: HKS5.20)

Investment Thesis

We believe that Xinyi is China's most diversified glass conglomerate. The defensive auto glass aftermarket busmess and the advanced low emission construction glass busmess continued to do well m 2013 with stable growth and steady margins. The key drivers for growth in the rest of this year is the recovery in margins for the float glass business. In the past, we have seen peak margms for float glass hit 40% when the market is in short supply. We could see margins recover when demand improves as new supply has slowed to a trickle smce the beginning of 2014.

Valuation

Our Dec-16 PT is based on a DCF valuation that assumes a market risk premium of 6.0% and a risk-free rate of 4.2% (yield on 10-year government notes in China). We have assumed a beta of 1.3 (Bloomberg Beta is 1.3). Accordingly, WACC is assumed at 11.4%. We have estimated free cash flow for XYG until 2020 and assume a terminal growth rate of 3.0%. The term mal growth is based on the annual growth rate expected in 2020 (the final year of the estimate period) subject to a minimum of 3% and a maximum of 6%, depending on the nature of the industry and the level of maturity in Chma.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

The key downside risks to our OW ratmg and PT are falling demand for construction glass that may result from a cooling property market in China and further weakness in PV glass demand from Europe.
