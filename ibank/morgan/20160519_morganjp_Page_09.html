Foreign exchange risk

The financial performance of the company, which is reported in USD terms, is exposed to foreign exchange risk. A depreciation of Rmb against USD adversely impacts revenue, which is mainly generated in the PRC. An appreciation of CAD against USD increases the costs of IMAX systems as IMAX Corp procures the equipment from a Canadian supplier. Negative impact from foreign exchange movements on financials may affect the share price, which is denominated in HKD terms, in a similar way.

Valuation and share price analysis

We adopt DCF valuation methodology to derive the company’s fair value. Our WACC assumption is 10.5%, based on a market risk premium of 7.0%, a risk-free rate of 4.0%, beta, of 1.05 and terminal growth rate of 2%.
