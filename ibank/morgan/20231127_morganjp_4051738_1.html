Japanese stocks have rallied in tandem with U.S. and European markets amid November’s rapid decline in U.S. interest rates and rise in the yen. Semiconductor names such as Tokyo Electron and tech stocks such as Recruit came back to life, and machinery stocks such as Keyence also pushed TOPIX higher. We view all of this as an initial reaction of risk assets to lower U.S. interest rates. As the market focus sliifts to the slowdown in the U.S. economy, we believe Japan’s structural changes will support Japanese equities.
