Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Sunnova Energy International Inc. (Overweight, Price Target. $47.00)

Investment Thesis

We rate NOVA Overweight. At~17x CY22E EVZEBITDA, we think the multiple is attractive set against an earnings growth rate of >60%. We expect NOVA to outperform the mean of our coverage over the next 6-12 months.

Valuation

Our Dec 2022 price target is $4 7, based on an assigned multiple of 18x our FY23E PF EBITDA (including loan principal and interest), adjusted for YE22 estimated net debt. The assigned multiple is a slight premium to those of distributed generation/SMid-cap software peers with long-term recurring subscription models, which we believe reflects NOVA’s higher growth rate.
