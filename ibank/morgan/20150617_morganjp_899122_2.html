Key catalysts for the stock price:

  • •    Life: Strong NBV growth potential on the back of the strong opening of the healthcare insurance market

  • •    Bank: Higher non-interest income growth potential, with increasing retail deposit growth leveraging -90M of the Group’s existing insurance policyholders


Upside risks to our view:

  • •    Top-line growth acceleration on the back of the protection-type life insurance market expansion

  • •    Better-than-expected asset quality deterioration in the banking/shadow banking sector in China.

  • •    More evidence of business differentiation among peers with strong evidence of business synergy between bank and insurance

Downside risks to our view:

  • •    Asset quality concerns at its banking and trust operations

  • •    Weaker-than-expected household demand for protection-type products, resulting in poor NBV growth

  • •    Larger-than-expected deterioration in the non-life underwriting cycle
