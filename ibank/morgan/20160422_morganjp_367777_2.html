Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

China Eastern Airlines - H (Overweight; Price Target: HKS6.00)

Investment Thesis

We believe CEA has the best geographical hub location which will benefit from Shanghai’s FTZ and Disneyland with sufficient aiiport capacity enabling the airline to expand its operations. Its fleet simplification and renewal will also help improve unit cost. Other positive drivers: margin upside from more premium traffic, rising travel penetration in China, yield recovery, traffic feed from Skyteam Alliance partners, enhanced co-operation with Delta and Qantas, liberalization of China's air links, low oil prices, industry consolidation.

Valuation

Our Jun-17 price target of HK$6.0 is based on 1.2x PB, in line with China Eastern’s historical average valuation since listing.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Downside risksinlucde: China’s economy weakens, rising competition from highspeed rail on domestic short-haul flights <2 hours, rising fuel prices, rising LCC competition, further weakening of the CNY.

Fu it her catalysts: Better-than-expected traffic and yield growth, falling jet fuel prices, Rmb rebound, earlier-than-expected success of its low-cost-carrier JV and a potential strategic investor.
