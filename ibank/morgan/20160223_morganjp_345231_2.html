Upcoming trade fair sales, with expectation for continued improvement in sales/operational momentum

Upside risks to our view:

  • (1)    Higher-than-expected improvement in sales

  • (1)    Significant downturn in the retail environment

  • (4)    Higher-than-expected sourcing costs

  • (5)    Higher-than-expected capex
