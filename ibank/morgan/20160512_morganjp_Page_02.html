Key catalyst for the stock price:

Continuing Rmb depreciation, weak China and lackluster traffic would drive the stock down.

Upside risks to our view:

Better than expected sales growth in gem-set sales, less pronounced normalization of gold sales and higher than expected margin improvements from shift in mix to non-gold products

Downside risks to our view:

HK and China sales decline continues for longer than expected, inability to keep cost plus model, FX impact and unexpected pressure on margins from promotions and costs may pose downside risk.
