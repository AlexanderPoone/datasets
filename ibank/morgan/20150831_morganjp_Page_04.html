We maintained sales estimate for 2015 and lifted 2016 sales to factor in higher MSG prices. We expect MSG to continue to rise in 2H15 (vs 1H15) as demand is usually stronger in the second half of the year. The reduction in EBIT in 2015 is mainly due to the weaker than expected performance in Xanthum Gum in 1H15 which should cany onto 2H15. However, we had already expected a sharp drop in Xanthum gum prices in 2016 in our previous estimates and as such we did not need to change 2016 margins as much. EBIT is lowered to factor in the lower GPM as well as higher GA costs. We added an extra loss of Rmb 60m related to the approx. Rmb2.0bn in overseas (mostly USD) debt following the recent depreciation of the Renminbi. Our latest 2015 EPS estimate is 20% lower than consensus expectations (consensus EPS = RmbO.455)
