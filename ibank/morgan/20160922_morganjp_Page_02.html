  • •    Astronger-than-expected improvement in pricing and margins as demand improves with the economy

  • •    Acceleration of capacity growth to foster more growth

Downside risks to our view:

  • •    New supply Io come online Io depress margins

  • •    Surge in cost of old cardboard due Io a booming US economy
