Table 1 : Sa Sa - HK/Macau SSSG

2Q17 and Golden Week Update

  • •    Golden Week SSSG +12.4%. For Golden Week Holiday from 1 to 7 October 2016, retail sales for HK/Macau recorded +13.8% growth and SSSG increased 12.4%. Number of transactions of Mainland Chinese customers increased 19.1 %, while average sales per transaction decreased 4.7%. We note that SSS for Golden Week 2015 declined 8.7% yoy, implying two year stack of +3.7%.

  • •    2Q17 SSSG -2.5%. Sa Sa also reported 2Q17 SSSG for its core HK & Macau market of-2.5% (vs 1Q17 -4.8%, 4Q16 -17.6%, 3Q16 -12.2%, 2Q16 -10.1%). Total turnover for HK & Macau retail declined 2.2%. Average sales per transaction declined 5.8% and total number of transactions increased 3.9%. For local HK customers, number of transactions increased by 1.8% (v 1Q17 -1.5%), while average sales per transaction decreased by 5.5% (v 1Q17 -7.1%). For Mainland Chinese customers, total number of transactions increased 5.9% (v 1Q17 +2.7%), while average sales per transaction decreased by 6.9% (v 1Q17 -6.4%). We expect improvement in total number of transactions to have partly come from Sa Sa's own proactive efforts to adjust its product offering towards more trendy Korean/Japanese products.
