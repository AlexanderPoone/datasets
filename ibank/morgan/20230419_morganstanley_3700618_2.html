MORGAN STANLEY RESEARCH Investigating Rates Seasonality Patterns April 2023


2023 update: Summary matrix of seasonal patterns in rates

  • •    This publication aims to track the seasonal patterns in EUR, UK and US rates. Those patterns could be linked to recurrent government supply dynamics or balance sheet activity from commercial banks. We update our statistics adding 2022 data, allowing the statistics to now cover the 2007-22 period.

  • •    Although we adopt a daily frequency approach, the purpose is not to track and trade the exact day of each period but use the results as an additional input to macroeconomic, market conditions and valuation inputs.
