Q-Strategy Help in EM

Stock screens that combine thematic macro and quant

  • •    Our advice is to position for a recovery in EM equities. The end of dollar strength and a likely delay in Fed tightening should take the pressure off EM currencies and provide greater policy flexibility to EM central banks. Our economists forecast more easing in India and ASEAN, and less tightening in Brazil, Mexico, Peru, Turkey and South Africa. Near-term Chinese risks have reduced with a stable CNY and strong credit support. Relative valuations and positioning further support the case of EM vs DM.

  • •    The correction presents a good opportunity to add to secular growth stocks (see Table 8), and our OW markets. The Bank of Indonesia cut rates for the second time this year. We remain OW (see Table 3). We upgraded Turkey to OW on 12 February (see Table 4). See Table 2 for high scoring stocks in India and Table 7 for Mexico.

  • •    Top 5 stocks combining Q-scores and top down macro strategy: BPCL, NetEase, Hanwha Chemical, Hanjay a Mandala and Qihoo.

  • •    Top 5 stocks based solely on composite Q-scores: Sappi, China Communications Construction. BPCL, NetEase and Truworths.
