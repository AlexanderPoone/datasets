FY23 result: The numbers

Revenue was +c. 1% yoy but higher costs saw EBIT -6% yoy, but c.2% ahead of our estimates. Net interest increased significantly to US$260m given balance sheet leverage and higher rates and matched guidance (of US$260-270m) partially offset by lower taxes which saw underlying NPAT -11% yoy (+3% vs. JPMe). FY23 DPS of US49cps was ahead of our estimates.

AMC’s Rev/EBIT largely matched our estimates with FY23 EPSof US73cps within management’s guidance range
