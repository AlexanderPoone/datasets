Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Tencent (Overweight; Price Target: HK$180.00)

Investment Thesis

Tencent delivered solid 1Q16 with outperformance in both revenue (3%/6% above JPMe/Consensus) and non-GAAP operating profit (4% above JPMe). On segmental basis, mobile gaming (+16% QoQ; 40% of total gaming revs) continued 4Q15 strength; yet, we note both brand ads/P4P ads (-23%/-13% QoQ) were adversely impacted by macro weakness as well as soft seasonality, esp. ecommerce category. We believe current market expectation on Tencent's ads performance could be demanding in contrast with the co's reiterated 'self-paced quality growth' strategy. Separately, other revenues (incl. payment, cloud, etc) ramped up quickly to 7% of total revs. Maintain OW.

Valuation

We value Tencent based on sum-of-the-parts approach, given the different attributes of business segments. Our SoTP delivers a total valuation of HK$l,705bn and a PT of HK$180. Key assumptions to our SoTP valuation include:

  • •    HK$358bn from PC games and online community sendees, based on 2016E net profit of HK$29.8bn and a P/E of 12x.

  • •    HK$460bn from m obile gam es, based on 2016E net profit of HK$ 18.8bn, a 16-18E net profit CAGR of 24% and a PEG of 1,0x.

  • •    HK$641 bn from online ads, based on 2016E net profit of HK$ 12.2bn, a 16-18E net profit CAGR of 52% and a PEG of 1,0x.

  • •    HK$92bn from equity investments, and

  • •    HK$ 155bn from net cash
