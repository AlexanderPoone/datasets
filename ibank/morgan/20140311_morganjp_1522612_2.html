Asia Pacific Equity Research 10 March 2014


Key catalysts for the stock price:

The company will release its 4Q14 sales release at the end of April 2014. Any positive or negative news will likely impact the share price.

Beyond the company’s 4Q14 sales release, the company will release its FY14 result around Jun-14. Any guidance for FY15will likely have an impact on the L'Occitane share price.


Upside risks to our view:

(1) Any upturn in the cosmetics retail environment in key geographic regions; (2) Better than expected customer acceptance of smaller brands for group; (3) Better than expected customer acceptance of new product launches; (4) Positive FX movements (weakening of Euro, or strengthening of Japanese Yen); (5) Lower than expected operating cost growth.


Downside risks to our view:

(1) Downturn in the retail environment for cosmetics globally; (2) Change in consumer tastes with respect to natural/organic cosmetics; (3) Any downturn in the retail environment of key geographic regions; (4) Higher than expected operating cost growth; (5) Adverse FX movements;

(6) Any events that negatively damage the brand image of the L’Occitane brand.
