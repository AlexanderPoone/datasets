Country Garden acquired Foshan land from Shui On

Views on the Estate: HK/China property monitor

Hong Kong / China Property team:

Cusson Leung (+852 2800 8526) / Ryan Li (+852 2800 8529) / Leo Ng (+852 2800 8522) / Karl Chan (+852 2800 8513)/ Jevon Jim (+852 2800 8538)

  • •    Country Garden acquired Foshan land from Shui On: On Apr 14. Shui On announced the sale of an undeveloped residential land (GFA: 231.500 sqm) in Foshan Lingnan Tiandi to Country Garden. The consideration is Rmb 1.726 mn. Additionally. Country Garden will settle the outstanding current accounts of Rmb 126 mn. making the total land AV to be ~Rmb8,000 psm. Net proceeds from the transaction amount to Rmb 1.803 mn. and it will be used in general financial purposes according to Shui On. (Source: HKEx)

J PM View: We think Country^ Garden (CG) remains aggressive in expansion, and as CG is keen to improve its brand name beyond tier-3 cities, they are try ing to market their brand by entering into more core areas in different cities. This could help the company's development in the longer term. At an AV of Rmb8,000 psm, we expect 23-25% gross margin from the project, which is in line with Country' Gardens current margin.

  • •    Second batch at Alassio to be launched with max. 4% price increment: Swire Properties has released the second price list on 30 units at Alassio (197 units) al Mid-Levels. This batch comprises unit sizes of 518-922 sf at a post-discount ASP of HKS21,441-HKS29.498 psf (vs HKS21.768-HKS26.921 psf in the first price list), representing a maximum increase of 4.2% over the first batch. The developer has also raised the prices of 10 unlaunched units included in the first price list by 2.7%-4.2% in order to align with the second price list. The second batch is expected to be launched early next week. On the other hand. NWD mentioned they may release the first price list on Bohemian House (191 units) al Sai Ying Pun next week, which will comprise al least 50 units.

Separately. Wharf/Nan Fung has sold the House 5 al Mount Nicholson Phi (17 units) al the Peak for HKS740 mn. or HKS80.672 psf. The house provides a saleable area of 9.173 sf. with 4.636 sf of patios as well as 1.530 sf of balcony and rooftop. The ASP al House 5 was 9% below that al House 6 sold in Feb-2016, and Wharf attributed the price difference to the variance in view and saleable area of the two houses. (Source: HKET)
