Proven track record of M&A capability

We think one of Fosun’s competitive advantages is acquiring valuable assets at reasonable prices and integrate its platform (see table below). It continues to invest and explore new business models. For example, in 2015 it acquired 9.73% equity interest in Guahao.com accumulatively and set up strategic cooperation with the company. It also invested RMB36 million in Mingyizhudao as a lead investor for its A-round financing for 15% of its equity interest in October 2015. It plans to cooperate with China life and Taikang group, major insurance companies in China, to build an “integrated insurance+ sendee +pharmaceuticals” platform.
