Raising HDT sales forecasts

Raising FYI6/17 HDT expectations to +19%/+7% with YTD growth supported by strong logistics demand while construction growth expected to pick up in coming months. Logistics truck demand (+25% Y/Y for 9M16) has led growth YTD, but with China property activity expected to pick up in 4Q16, we anticipate sequential improvement in sales growth of construction HDTs. Looking ahead to FYI 7, we anticipate logistics demand to continue to be a key growth driver while expecting replacement demand from trucks bought during the peak years (2010/11) to begin to kick in.
