Tencent

Gaming solid as portfolio diversified; ads facing macro headwinds

Tencent delivered solid 1Q16 with outperformance in both revenue (3%/6% above JPMe/consensus) and non-GAAP operating profit (4% above JPMe). On a segmental basis, mobile gaming (+16% QoQ; 40% of total gaming revs) continued 4Q15’s strength; yet, we note both brand ads/P4P ads (-23%/-13% QoQ) were adversely impacted by macro weakness as well as soft seasonality, especially ecommerce category. We believe current market expectation on Tencent's ads performance could be demanding in contrast with the company’s reiterated “self-paced quality growth” strategy. Separately, other revenues (incl. payment, cloud, etc.) ramped up quickly to 7% of total revenues. Maintain OW.

  • •    Strong momentum of mobile games continued, which generated RMB8.2bn revenue in 1Q16 (+16% QoQ/+86% YoY; 14% above JPMe) driven by introduction of new hard-core genres (e.g., shooting and action games) and a sound pipeline. We expect Tencent to sustain sanguine growth of its gaming business with an enriched mobile game portfolio (84 titles in IQ) and strong mobile games pipeline (e.g. based on popular third-party PC games IPs including Woool, JX, ZT Online, etc). We revise our 2016 smartphone games revenue forecast up by 3% to RMB30.5bn (+43% YoY).

  • •    Soft tone on ads outlook; expect market expectation to be re-based. Ads revenue missed JPMe by 19% in 1Q16 on macros, unfavorable seasonality of ecommerce ads as well as base effect. Management sounded conservative on ads outlook into 2Q, noting continuing weakness in ecommerce sector whereas the company’s dedicated focus on ecosystem development and quality rather than focusing on monetizing Moments Ads in a hurry only for short-term gains, especially for Weixin Moments. We expect market expectation on ads, particularly P4P ads, to be re-based (we cut FYI6 ads revenue forecast by 7% to RMB26.8bn, representing +53% YoY or 20% of total revenues).

  • •    Maintain OW and tweak down PT to HKS180. PT implies a 2016E P/E of 32x.
