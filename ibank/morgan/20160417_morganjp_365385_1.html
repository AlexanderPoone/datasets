1Q16 preview: Improving topline, diverging bottom line

We expect CM and CT to report strong topline growth and slight earnings growth for IQ 16, which in our view are likely to receive good market reaction. Despite a significant yoy drop in CU's earnings, CU's mobile business is likely to show an improving trend with a minimal yoy decline in mobile service revenue and a small yoy increase in ARPU, reaffirming our expectation for mobile service revenue turnaround in 2Q16 and sequential improvement in earnings in the coming quarters.

  • •    Improving ARPU and mobile service trend: We forecast low single digit mobile ARPU growth across the board on a yoy basis off a low base in IQ 15, on the back of strong data usage growth and rational pricing dynamics. This, together with accelerating subscriber growth, should lead to 10% and 5% yoy mobile service revenue growth in CT and CM respectively according to our estimates. Due to CU's yoy subscriber decline, we estimate a small 1% yoy decline in its mobile service revenue in IQ 16, indicating the operator is on track to achieve mobile revenue turnaround in 2QI6.

  • •    Diverging EBITDA trends: We estimate 4% yoy EBITDA growth for CM, and a decline of 1% and 20% in CT and CU's EBITDA respectively. Tower usage fees have a more meaningful impact in % terms on CU and CT than CM, and therefore put more pressure on margins for the two. CU's much weaker EBITDA trend was also due to a yoy increase in S&M expenses, minimal top-line growth and also a high base in 1Q15.

  • •    Stable earnings for CM&CT, CU significant drop: Despite CM's better EBITDA growth, we project a similarly small increase in earnings for both CM and CT, as CM's more aggressive 4G depreciation schedule means the tower-related savings cannot fully offset the increase in 4G capex. We estimate an 80%-90% yoy tumble in CU's earnings off a high base of 1Q15 earnings, due to the significant decline in EBITDA. Given its improving operational trend and high operating leverage, we expect meaningful sequential improvement in CU's earnings in the coming quarters. CM, CU and CT will report 1Q16 results on 20th, 21st and 28th of April, respectively.
