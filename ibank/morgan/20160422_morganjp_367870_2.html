Key catalysts for the stock price:

Pick up in container shipping volumes, industry’s capacity rationalization plans could increase the success of future rate hike plans, new vessels have lower unit cost structure, industry alliances/M&A.

Upside risks to our view:

Lower fuel prices, global demand stronger than expected, liners succeed in raising freight rates.

Downside risks to our view:

Industry loses capacity discipline, global container shipping demand weakens further, freight rates decline, rising fuel prices and limited pass-through, prolonged industry oversupply.
