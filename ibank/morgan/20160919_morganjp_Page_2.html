Investment Thesis

We are OW on Mengniu as we believe that the risk of margin erosion on the back of raw milk oversupply is lower now given that the company has delivered margin expansion even during the difficult 1H16 and that oversupply issue is likely to ease as we get closer to the end of the year. As near-term risks appear to be over, we are now focusing on mid-to-long-term drivers: i) margin expansion for liquid milk business on the back of product mix improvement; h) Yashili and Modem Dany earnings appear to have bottomed out and should start helping consolidated margins.

Valuation

Our Dec-17 PT of HK$ 17 is based on 19.5x one-year forward P/E, which is in line with the historical mean.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Key downside risks to our rating and price target include higher-than-expected A&P spending, weaker-than-expected UHT milk or irrational competition which may cause a significant increase in opex as a percentage of revenue and hurt Mengniu’s margins. Food safety is also a potential risk. Key upside risks include a higher contribution from the opportunity category, which may help margin enhancement.
