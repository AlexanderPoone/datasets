Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Sa Sa International Holdings Limited (Neutral; Price Target: HKS2.85)

Investment Thesis

While tourism trends seem to be improving, we expect margins to remain under pressure for the short to medium term for Sa Sa. Furthermore, we believe there is still uncertainty with respect to the HK retail environment. We await more datapoints for Sa Sa before turning more positive.

Valuation

Our Mar-17 PT of HK$2.85 is based on a P/E of ~15x our earnings forecasts for the year ending Mar-18. Our target P/E is a discount to Sa Sa’s historical five-year average P/E to reflect the risk of de-rating, given slowing earnings momentum and the risk of consensus earnings forecast reductions.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Upside risks include: (1) an upturn in Mainland Chinese tourism in Hong Kong; (2) an upturn in customer purchasing behavior within Sa Sa stores; (3) lower-than-expected operating expenses; (4) better-than-expected company execution at the store level; and (5) better-than-expected cross-border e-commerce and 020 initiatives.
