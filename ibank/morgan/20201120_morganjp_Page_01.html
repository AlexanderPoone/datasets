Alibaba Pictures

Improving profitability despite revenue pressure, marketing going live

Alibaba Pictures’ 1HFY21 (Apr20-Sep20) revenue was suppressed by a nationwide cinema shutdown before mid-July, but losses narrowed thanks to stringent cost control. The company recently launched a campaign to leverage live broadcasting for online promotion and distribution, and targets operating income breakeven in FY21. We tweak our Dec-21 PT from HK$1.20 to HK$1.15 and remain Neutral due to relatively demanding valuations.

  • •    1HFY21 revenue affected by cinema closure: Total revenue decreased 38% yoy, to Rmb927mn. Internet-based promotion and distribution (P&D) was the main drag (-68% yoy), as a nationwide theatre closure before midJuly and a yoy decline in box office after reopening led to a drop in online ticketing revenue. Content production revenue grew nicely, by 87% yoy, on the back of increased sales of self-produced dramas to Youku. Integrated development grew 40% yoy, decelerating from -120% growth in FY20.

  • •    Losses narrowed with stringent cost control: Gross margin came down 22ppts yoy, to 38%, as the theatre shutdown led to a decline of high-margin online ticketing revenue. The S&M expense to revenue ratio came down to 7% from 44%/26% in 1HFY20/2HFY20 due partly to reduced ticket subsidies and less marketing expenditure during the lockdown. Profit (gross profit minus allocable S&M expense) was Rmb285mn in 1HFY21 vs. Rmb248mn in 1HFY20, as the turnaround of the content production business offset weakness in online P&D. Net loss decreased to Rmbl62mn in 1HFY21 from Rmb390mn in 1HFY20 due mainly to S&M expense reduction. Management aims at operating income breakeven in FY21.

  • •    Content production business update: Movies: Alipics has released three movies under Jincheng Collaboration Plan since 2018 (Peppa Celebrates Chinese New Year, Love You Forever, Coffee or Tea?), and all are profitmaking. It plans to release 20 Jincheng movies, in which it is the lead producer/distributor, in the next fom years, and 13 are completed/under preparation. Dramas: Over the past three years, five dramas produced by Alipics have been streamed, with another nine delivered and pending release. Nine projects are now under preparation. It targets delivering 10 dramas inFY21.

  • •    Online P&D with live broadcasting: Alipics recently launched an MCN (Multi-Cliannel Network) project that leverages live broadcasting for P&D. Partnersliips have been formed with short video platforms such as Douyin, Kuaishou and Taobao Live Broadcasting. Since commencement in June, it has signed contracts with 178 KOLs, with over 300mn users registered. Video view counts have exceeded 30bn.

  • •    Management sees a low risk of cannibalization between theatres and online streaming platforms. Blockbusters will go for theatrical release, and small-budget comedies/classic movies can be released online. Alipics will expand investment in web movies in the coming years.
