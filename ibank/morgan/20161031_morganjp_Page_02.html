Key catalysts for the stock price:

  • •    Further policy support on NEV

  • •    Launch of NEV models In 2016-17

Upside risks to our view:

  • •    NEV units - government hitting the 2020 sales target - our assumption implies a one-year lag

  • •    NEV GM - BYD able to up-sell its NEV to higher price, or cut battery costs faster than expected

  • •    EV battery/energy storage - BYD starting to sell EV batteries to other auto makers, and scaling up its solar/ battery/ controller for ESS one-stop shop

Downside risks to ourview:

  • •    Missing estimates - bumpy learning curve for various parties including bus companies/ consumers

  • •    Product hiccups - BYD running into product recall/ battery warranty Issues, as seen In other auto brands

  • •    Competition - global auto brands/ battery makers ramping up shipments in China production plants
