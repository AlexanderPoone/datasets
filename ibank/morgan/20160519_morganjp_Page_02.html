  • •    Beneficiary of China's booming box office.

  • •    IMAX film's box office beats expectations.

  • •    More-than-expected Chinese language IMAX films.

Downside risks to our view:

  • •    Dolby gains stronger-than-expected customer traction and affects demand for IMAX.
