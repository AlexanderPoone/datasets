Asia Pacific Equity Derivatives Highlights

Long KOSPI2 FVA, KOSPI2 calls contingent on SPX capped, Volatility positioning and dynamics

  • •    Lon» KOSPI2 FVA: We recommend investors re-load long exposure to KOSPI2 volatility via FVA after months of declines inequity implied volatility. We attribute the recent weakness inequity implied volatility to optimismo na dovish pivot in central bank rate policies and reduction in China/EM Asia market risk premium. We think both factors have likely run their course. While shorter dated volatility appears cheaper to buy, we remain wary of a lack of realized volatility as equity markets remain in the process of finding a clear direction. We prefer FVA to position for a potential reset higher in longer dated implied volatility. Apart from the macro-economic considerations, the case of buying KOSPI200 FVA is stronggiven attractive entry levels, imbalanced vega supply/demand dynamics in favor of FVA buyers and country specific catalysts. We recommend investors buying KOSPI2 Dec23 - Dec24 325 FVA straddle.

  • •    Buy call spread on KOSPI2 contingent on SPX capped: Expectation of Korea being add ed to MSCI watch list for a potential upgrad e to DM market status increased following a slew ofannouncements made by the country’s financial regulators in late January 2023. We think the potential of Korea being placed on the watch list will provide a boost to investor sentiment. The optimism around a Korea market status upgrade is partly responsible for a pickup in KOSPI 200 short-dated upside volatility. Considering recent moves in short dated volatility, we recommend investors buying KOSPI 200 Jul23 call spread contingent on SPX capped to position for the June 2023 MSCI market classification review.
