Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Hong Kong & China Gas (Underweight; Price Target: HK$12.30)

Investment Thesis

Hong Kong & China Gas is the largest downstream gas distributor in Hong Kong and China, with its monopoly position in HK and 100+ city-gas projects in China. The company is also involved in upstream energy and midstream projects in China. It also owns a 15.8% stake in the IFC complex in HK.

We have an UW on HKCG because this is a company with slowing EPS growth to ~5% in the long run. Yet, its valuation is >30% above peers’, which is unjustified because its peers’ growth rates are at 2-3%. Negative catalysts could come from regulatory risks in China and weaker-than-expected gas consumption in HK and China.

Valuation

Our Jun-17 PT for HKCG is determined based on a sum-of-the-parts valuation, using DCF to value the HK, China and New Energy segments and NAV to value the Property segment. Our PT implies FY17E P/E of 22x and yield of 3.1%

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Upside risks include: (1) strong gas demand in HK and China, which would result in higher revenue; (2) higher oil prices which would result in stronger-than-expected earnings from upstream projects; and (3) share buybacks, which would result in share price increases.
