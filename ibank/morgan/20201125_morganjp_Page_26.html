Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Anadolu Efes Biracilik ve Malt Sanayii AS (Overweight; Price Target: TL31.70)

Investment Thesis

We are upgrading Anadolu Efes from N to OW with a new Dec-21 PT of TL31.7.. The company is winner of opening up economies thanks to its relatively high exposure to on premise which is 25% of sales in Turkey, 5-10% in CIS countries and small at 3% in Russia. The company has successfully strengthened its market position in Russia where depressed beer margins have already bottomed out and likely to rebound in the medium term given implemented cost savings and growing volumes. Shares are attractively priced, offering deep discount of 49% vs global bottlers on trailing EV/EBITDA multiple. Strong cash generation capacity as well as potential to improve beer margins should help the stock to narrow the discount to peers.

Valuation

We use a sum-of-the-parts valuation methodology to arrive at Anadolu Efes’ total value of TL31.7. We have multiple-DCF blended valuation for businesses, where we set target 2021 EBITDA multiple for beer operations at 8x that is 20% discount to European peers. Our PT implies 2021E EV/EBITDA of 4.2x when adjusted with stake in CCOLA. This is still a low multiple vs peers and any potential stabilization in beer margins at higher level may unlock the value in shares.

Risks to Our Rating and Price Target

The main downside risks to our view would be:

  • •    Lower-than-anticipated recovery in the Turkish beer market due to delay in recovery in tourism sector as well as deteriorating affordability of beer products post tax hikes.

  • •    Bad weather conditions could drive lower demand for beer and soft drinks, thereby could put pressure on revenues and margins.

  • •    Intensified competition in Russia may limit improvement we see in international margins in the medium term.
