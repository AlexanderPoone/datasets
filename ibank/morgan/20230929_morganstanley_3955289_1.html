Staring Into The Abyss

Whoever owns duration should see to it that in the process they do not become blinded by their ownership. If we gaze long enough into the abyss, we know the abyss will gaze back at us. While we prepare to break our gaze, we stare for a bit longer -staying long duration and the USD.

Global Macro Strategy

We discuss the Last set of US government economic data we may see for a period, take the temperature of term premiums in the US Treasury market, discuss whether BTPs are the weakest Link in the euro area, and cover implications of India's inclusion in the GBI-EM Index.

Interest Rate Strategy

We maintain Long 5y USTs, Long 30y TIPS, and Long SFRZ4 on SFRU3Z4Z5 fLy. We maintain our EUR 1Oy1Oy rec and our Dec 23 mid curve 96.75/97.OO/97.25/97.5O call condor. On the curve, we keep our flatteners on EUR 2s10s and 30s50s swap. On cash, we keep our short 5y Bono vs. 20y EU, while we close our short BTP May 33 vs. Bund Aug 32, our Long 10y Spain vs. OAT/PGB, and the 10s30s BTP/Spain Box. On ASW, we maintain the 10s30s OAT ASW box (short 30y OAT) and our conditional Bund ASW widener. We close JGB 20s30s flattener and the receive 10y TONA OIS position. We newly enter TONA OIS 2s5s steepener (DV011.5 vs 1) and Long 5y JGB vs pay 7y OIS (DV01 2 vs 1) for the attractive carry trade.

Currency & Foreign Exchange

Stay Long USD versus risk-sensitive EMs and add Long USD/CHF. Risknegativity is a necessary condition for falling US yields to keep USD strength. We expect that the direct impact of a US government on G10 FX will be muted. Oil price pass-through to CAD and NOK is a function of the shape of the oil futures curve. Stay short AUD/NlZD ahead of the RBA and RBNZ meetings.

Inflation-Linked Bonds

In the US, we discuss the implications of a potential government shutdown on inflation products. We maintain our Long 30y TIPS trade, and our widening 20y iota bias.

Short-Duration Strategy

We maintain short 2y SOFR swap spread and Long SFRZ4 on U3Z4Z5 fly.
