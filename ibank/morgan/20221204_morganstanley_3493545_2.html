Investment Thesis for Focus List Additions

Adding ANTA Sports Products (2O2O.HK) to the APxJ, GEM, and China/HK Focus Lists

Morgan Stanley analyst Dustin Wei thinks ANTA is a good reopening play, coupled with a compelling long-term investment thesis:

  • •    ANTA and FILA brands’retail saleswill start lapping a Low base from 2Q23, which is likely to be the timing of greater consumption recovery, given the current pace of reopening.

  • •    In particular, Fl LA's recovery could beat the market's expectation. Some investors think FILA is stuck in a structural downturn, but we think Fl LA's sales were affected mainly by its high concentration in Tier 1 and 2 cities.

  • •    The company’s initiatives to improve ANTA and FILA were disrupted by Covid. Following gradual reopening, we think their improvements will accelerate. The focus will be ANTA's upgrade in brand/product and expansion in higher-tier cities; FILA's push on athletic products and footwear; and product portfolio optimization

  • •    The company’s emerging brands - Arc'teryx, Salomon, Wilson, Descente and Kolon - should contribute to ANTA's valuation more significantly in the next 3-5 years.
