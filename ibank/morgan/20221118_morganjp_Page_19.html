Recovery Rates for HY Bonds and Loans

Recovery rates for high-yield bonds are slightly below their record high (2004’s 58.5%). Meanwhile, recovery rates for loans declined m/m and are below their historical average. Specifically, loan-only issuers Cineworld and Phoenix Services International were included in the LTM calculation and had below average recoveries. Specifically, recovery rates for high-yield bonds are 56.5% over the last 12 months, 17%-pts above the 25-year annual average of 39.9%.
