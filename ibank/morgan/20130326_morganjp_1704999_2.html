Company Description

Agile Property (3383.HK) is one of the leading hornebuilders in China. Based in Guangdong, the Group focuses on large-scale residential community developments and is ranked one of the top real estate developers in Guangdong Province in terms of sales. As of March 2013, Agile has a total land bank of 35 million sqm in Guangzhou. Zhongshan and other Guangdong cities, as well as Nanjing, Chengdu, Shenyang, Xi’an, Changzhou. Agile also has an investment property portfolio in Shanghai, Sanya and Guangdong Province.

Price target and valuation analysis

Our Dec-13 PT is based on 7.5x FY13 P/E. Our target P/E is in line with the mid-cap average, as we believe Agile’s margin provides a significant buffer against adverse market conditions, offset by slow sales Agile has achieved year-to-date.
