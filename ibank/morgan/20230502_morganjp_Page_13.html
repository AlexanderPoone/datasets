GEM funds - increasingly UW, but nothing out of the ordinary

Looking at T iirkiye through the lens of EM funds positioning, we see that T iirkiye is still in the middle of the EM pack in terms of OW and UWs- at least comparing it to other small (i.e. sub-2%) EM markets. Tiirkiye is only 0.6% of the EM index - having been >3x the size 10 years ago, more than 2% of the index. In the early ‘10’s, Tiirkiye was a huge consensus OW among EM markets. And it has receded from EM fund portfolios. Now, slightly more than half of EM funds hold zero in Tiirkiye, 35 out of 56 funds - similar to similar-sized markets like the Philippines, or small Hungary, but more popular than bigger Malaysia or Qatar.

Tiirkiye average weightings relative to MSCI benchmark and net OW/ UW

Tiirkiye does stand out among EM funds in one respect: there are a few funds with substantial OW’s. The fund with the biggest position in Tiirkiye holds 5.2% of its fund in Tiirkiye, a 4.6% OW. Tiirkiye has a few big fans among EM investors.
