Limited visibility on NT catalysts; cloud and mobile games materialization still a way off

We assume coverage of Kingsoft with a Neutral rating and Dec-16 PT of HK$16.5. We believe Kingsoft is still in an investment stage, with focus gradually shifting from mobile internet to Cloud and mobile games. With investments in mobile internet gradually bearing fruit, we believe Kingsoft will continue to benefit from its CMCM holdings (47%), with Cheetah revenue contribution to reach 71% /75% in FY16/17E vs. 64%/50% in 2015/14 (JPMe). We are constructive on the company's efforts in mobile games and cloud, but with limited visibility on the timing of materializing revenue contribution. We see potential margin improvement due to a more balanced investment strategy in Cheetah and operating leverage.

  • •    Cheetah Mobile to remain the key growth driver for the group in the next few years. We expect Kingsoft to benefit meaningfully from its Cheetah Mobile holding. We expect Cheetah to quadruple its annual mobile revenue to RMBlOJbn (92% of Cheetah's total revenue) by FY18E (15-18E CAGR of 61%), driven by 1) —50% growth of mobile MAU (JPMe from 609m by YE15 to over 900m by YE18), 2) nearly 3x improvement in mobile monetization efficiency as measured by revenue per MAU.

  • •    Mobile gaming: a latecomer but possible to catch up. Kingsoft lagged during the boom of mobile games market over the past two years. However, we note potential upside in its mobile gaming performance in 2016/17 given 1) new mobile games to enter beta testing in early 2016, based on Kingsoft’s popular legacy PC games IPs (JX World and JX III), 2) recent exclusive jointoperations partnership with Tencent on JX series Mobile. We estimate max. 5%/8% revenue upside from Tencent partnership for FY16/17E providing the launch becomes a blockbuster hit (e.g. monthly grossing above RMB 100m).

  • •    Cloud business ramps up quickly from a low base but immaterial in near term. We think the emerging segment is unlikely to move the needle yet but will remain a key investment focus in the coming years.

  • •    SoTP valuation generates a PT of HKS16.5, which includes HK$6.5 from Cheetah Mobile, HK$2.1 from online games, HK$3.4 from WPS and others, HK$1 from investments and HK$ 3.5 from net cash position.
