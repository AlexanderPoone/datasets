Investment Thesis, Valuation and Risks

Melco International (Neutral; Price Target: HK$13.00)

Investment Thesis

We remain Neutral on Melco IntT (Melco) with a Dec-17 PT of HK$13.0.

Melco shares are up 5% YTD (vs the sector up 32% on average) and have been the worst performer in Macau gaming. We do feel the underperformance is overdone (hence we see upside to Melco shares, amidst our broadly positive sector view). But, we are fundamentally less enthusiastic about this stock (vs. our OW-rated names), as we are wary thatMPEL’s properties could face tougher competition from openings such as City of Dream vs. Wynn Palace & MGM Cotai (opening 2Q17E), and Studio City vs. Parisian.

Also, the stock currently trades at a 36% discount to NAV, compared to historical average of 38%; this looks reasonable to us and we rate Melco as Neutral.

Valuation

Our Dec-17 PT ofHK$13.0 is derived via SOTP, with a PT of US$17.0 for Melco Crown (covered by our US Gaming Analyst Joseph Greff), the market price for other holdings, and net cash. We apply a holding discount of 25%.

Risks to Rating and Price Target

Downside risks include: 1) RMB devaluation could dampen gaming demand from China & could further tighten liquidity within junket system; 2) unfavorable regulatory changes; and 3) slower-than-expected ramp-up of Studio City.

Upside risks include: 1) Potential policy support (such as policy loosening, allowing smoking lounges inside casinos, etc.); 2) stronger-than-expected improvement in industiy demand; 3) faster-than-expected ramp-up in Studio City.
