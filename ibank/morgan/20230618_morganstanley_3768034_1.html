Asia Sustainability Mid-Year Outlook I Asia Pacific

Revisiting the Three Themes and What's Next

Our three themes to drive alpha and impact remain intact. Investors should focus on China's SOEs, Japan's ESG ideas, and India's transition, as these areas should benefit from a renewed focus on governance and a multipolar world. We also discuss areas of focus for China, Japan and Australia.

Key Stock Ideas for 2H2O23 - See Exhibit 1 for the full stock list

  • 1)    Japan ESG ideas: Japan is our strategist's top pick on the back of macro, micro and multipolar drivers. On the ESG front, we recommend green enablers and governance improvers, both are aligned with our ESG rate of change thesis.

  • 2)    Selectively buy China SOEs, with additional screens on ESG disclosure as we believe SOEs with ESG disclosures will be in a better position to attract global ESG funds based on their reform progress. On top of our ESG disclosures screen, investors may wish to incorporate further screens on ESG risks and discussions (e.g., UN Global Compact violations) as appropriate for their portfolios.

  • 3)    India's transition: If sentiment in China does not improve, we think investors could potentially Look for ESG names in India as another significant market for ESG transition opportunities.

What's new about our three themes? See Exhibit 2 for a general overview of key ESG issues, impacts and time horizons when the effects may materialise.

Theme #1 - The multipolar world trend will put more focus on "ESG Rate of Change”: The trend continues amid geopolitical tension and more investors appreciating the regional differences of ESG. Our sustainable solution, with updated CAPEX data can help investors identify ESG RoC stocks.

Theme #2- Due diligence rules, carbon pricesand physical risks potentially disrupting supply chains Advancement in carbon border adjustment mechanisms and corporate sustainability due diligence directives will further impact APAC supply chains. Carbon pricing is developing in Japan and China. We expect heightened focus on climate adaptation to hedge against more frequent and severe weather events, especially ahead of COP28 Later this year.

Theme #3 - Continued regulatory focus on definitions and transparency of sustainable finance: There will be updates on ISSB and US SEC requirements Later this year. Sustainability disclosures enhancements are also in the works in Hong Kong, Australia, India and Taiwan.
