Investment Thesis

We believe the underperformance of Wharf is overdone. Its current valuation is pricing in an extreme decline in retail sales in Hong Kong when we believe the pricing power of the shopping mall landlords is still intact. We believe the stock will re-rate from the current level on the back of the continuous improvement in the China residential market and Hong Kong property launches.

Valuation

Our Dec-16 price target of HKS56.6 is based on a 37% discount to NAV, which is 0.5 S.D. below its long-term average.
