Tissues to grow to a meaningful business by 2020

We summarize below our expectations for LMP’s tissue business. This is based on management’s plans to complete 575,000 tons of annual capacity per year by the end of 2017 as well as achieve HK$1000 net profit per ton when these lines are running smoothly at a reasonably full utilization rates. Our long-term expectations are for the company to reach 1,015,000 tons per year in annual capacity by the end of 2020 and to have net profit per ton ranging from HK$877 to HK$951 for the 2016 to 2020 period. We expect net profits from the tissue business to grow from 3% of total net profits in 2016 to 17% of total net profits by 2020.
